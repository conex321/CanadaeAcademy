# Course Catalog — courses.html

A comprehensive, filterable course catalog page for Canada e Academy — Canada's interactive online high school. The page should feel like a modern SaaS product catalog with smart filtering and clear course cards. Must display 75+ courses across Grades 7-12 with grade-based filtering, ESL section, language courses, upgrade courses, free courses, and coming soon courses.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first, responsive
- Background: Clean White (#ffffff) main, Soft Cool Gray (#f5f7fa) for alternate sections
- Primary Accent: Deep Blue (#2c3892) for headers, filters, active states, links
- Secondary Accent: Canadian Red (#ed1c23) for Register CTAs and highlights
- Text Primary: Near Black (#1a1a2e) for headings
- Text Secondary: Warm Gray (#6b7280) for body/descriptions
- Font: Inter, semi-bold (600) headlines, regular (400) body, line-height 1.7
- Buttons: 8px radius, hover lift. Primary Deep Blue, secondary Canadian Red
- Cards: 12px radius, Soft Cool Gray background, hover shadow+lift
- Navigation: Sticky white header — logo left, nav center, "Register Now" red CTA right
- Footer: Deep Blue (#2c3892) bg, white text, BSID #665804, tagline "Your Path. Your Pace. Your Future."

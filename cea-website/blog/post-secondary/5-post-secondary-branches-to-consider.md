# 5 Post-Secondary Branches You Should Consider

High school is a time when you really start to think about your future. This can be stressful, especially if you are not sure what career path you want to follow. At [Canada e Academy](https://canadaeacademy.com), we do our best to help all of our students choose the path and courses that work best for them. In this article, we will list five potential post-secondary pathways you can take and the courses that will help you along the way.

## 1. Business Studies

Studying business is a great way to set yourself up for all types of career paths. Whether you plan to join a pre-existing company, work at a start-up, or create your own company, a degree in business can be beneficial.

Canada is home to some of the best business programmes in the world, such as Rotman School of Management at the University of Toronto, Smith School of Business at Queen's, Ivey Business School at Western, or Desautels Faculty of Management at McGill.

**Key Grade 12 courses:**
- [MHF4U — Advanced Functions](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/mhf4u)
- [MCV4U — Calculus and Vectors](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/mcv4u)
- [ENG4U — English](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/eng4u)
- [BBB4M — International Business Fundamentals](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/bbb4m)
- [BOH4M — Business Leadership](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/boh4m)

## 2. Engineering

When it comes to Engineering careers, the possibilities are vast — from Environmental and Biomedical engineers to Systems, Electrical, Chemical, and Aerospace engineering and beyond.

**Key Grade 12 courses:**
- MHF4U — Advanced Functions
- MCV4U — Calculus and Vectors
- ENG4U — English
- [SPH4U — Physics](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/sph4u)
- [SCH4U — Chemistry](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/sch4u)

## 3. Health and Life Sciences

There are many fields to choose from if you are interested in Health or Life Sciences. Whether you are looking into the medical field, veterinary science, or research in areas like microbiology, several core classes will help you build the foundational knowledge you need.

**Key Grade 12 courses:**
- MHF4U — Advanced Functions
- MCV4U — Calculus and Vectors
- ENG4U — English
- [SBI4U — Biology](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/sbi4u)
- SCH4U — Chemistry

## 4. Computer Science

In the modern age, we are more reliant on technology than ever. Getting a degree in Computer Science opens up several career possibilities, with cities like Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, and Waterloo all emerging as major tech hubs in Canada.

**Key Grade 12 courses:**
- MHF4U — Advanced Functions
- MCV4U — Calculus and Vectors
- ENG4U — English
- SPH4U — Physics
- [ICS4U — Computer Science](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/ics4u)

## 5. Arts and Social Sciences

Studying the humanities is another post-secondary pathway that can lead to a wide variety of career paths. Most programmes require ENG4U Grade 12 University English and five other Grade 12 U/M courses.

**Arts options:** [ADA4M — Drama](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/ada4m), [AVI4M — Visual Arts](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/avi4m), [AWQ4M — Photography](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/awq4m)

**Social Sciences options:** [CGW4U — Canadian and World Issues](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/cgw4u), [CHY4U — World History](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/chy4u), [CLN4U — Canadian and International Law](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/cln4u), [CPW4U — Canadian and International Politics](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/cpw4u), [HZT4U — Philosophy](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/hzt4u)

The five pathways listed above are just a few of the options you have when it comes to your post-secondary planning. Whether you intend to take one of these pathways or do something else, [Canada e Academy](https://canadaeacademy.com) is here to help.

# Benefits of Upgrading High School Marks Online

Whether you are a recent high school graduate or an adult learner looking to change career paths, upgrading your high school marks can be a powerful step toward achieving your goals. At [Canada e Academy](https://canadaeacademy.com/), we offer [upgrade courses](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/upgrade-courses) that allow students to retake courses they have already completed to improve their grades.

## Why Upgrade Your Marks?

### Meet Post-Secondary Requirements

Many university and college programmes have minimum grade requirements for admission. If your current marks fall short of these requirements, upgrading allows you to improve your grades and strengthen your application.

### Change Your Academic Pathway

Perhaps you completed college-level courses in high school but have since decided to pursue a university programme that requires university-level prerequisites. Upgrading allows you to take the courses you need without repeating your entire high school education.

### Compete for Scholarships

Many scholarships are awarded based on academic performance. Improving your grades through upgrading can make you a more competitive candidate for scholarships and bursaries, potentially saving you thousands of dollars in post-secondary costs.

### Refresh Your Knowledge

If it has been several years since you completed high school, an upgrade course can help refresh your knowledge and prepare you for the academic demands of post-secondary study.

## The Online Advantage

Upgrading your marks online offers several benefits compared to returning to a traditional classroom:

- **Flexibility:** Study on your own schedule without disrupting work or family commitments
- **Self-paced learning:** Take the time you need to truly master the material
- **Convenience:** Study from anywhere with an internet connection
- **Support:** Access to qualified Ontario Certified Teachers who provide personalised feedback

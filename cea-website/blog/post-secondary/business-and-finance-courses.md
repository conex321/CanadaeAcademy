# Understanding Business and Finance: High School Courses to Help You Succeed

In today's modern economic and social climate, understanding business and how it works is an essential skill to help you navigate not just the workforce but higher education and your personal life as well.

Whether you are interested in a career in business or finance, starting your own company or brand, or just want a better understanding of how to navigate your own finances, there are many courses you can take at the high school level to help you learn about the world of business and finance. At Canada e Academy, we have several courses at different grade levels to help in all different areas of the business world.

## Grade 9 and 10 Courses

**[Grade 9 Building the Entrepreneurial Mindset (BEM1O)](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/bem1o)** is a great course to get started on your business journey. Students learn what makes an entrepreneur thrive, covering topics including developing an entrepreneurial mindset, financial literacy and budgeting, and business communication and branding.

**[Grade 10 Launching and Leading a Business (BEP2O)](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/bep2o)** helps students build their marketing and customer relations, financial planning, and business technology skills. Students also learn about operations and human resources management, both of which are vital branches of business.

## Grade 11 Courses

**[Grade 11 Financial Accounting Fundamentals (BAF3M)](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/baf3m)** — This course focuses on the financial and accounting aspects of business. Students develop financial analysis and decision-making skills, as well as an understanding of computerised accounting and how to process accounts for service and merchandise-based businesses.

**[Grade 11 Introduction to Entrepreneurship (BDI3C)](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/bdi3c)** — This course focuses on further building your entrepreneurial skills. Students learn how to recognise opportunities for new business ventures, how to organise their resources, and create a solid business plan.

**[Grade 11 Marketing (BMI3C)](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/bmi3c)** — One of the most important parts of business is marketing. This course teaches students about the consumer, market research, product development, branding, pricing, distribution and logistics, as well as advertising and sales.

**[Grade 11 The Individual and the Economy (CIE3M)](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/cie3m)** — This course specifically focuses on the issues and challenges facing the Canadian economy, how the economy works as a whole, and how current economic practices can influence the economy in the future.

## Grade 12 Courses

**[Grade 12 Financial Accounting Principles (BAT4M)](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/bat4m)** — Accounting is essentially the language of business. This course builds on the foundational knowledge from Grade 11, getting into more detailed and complex theories and practices to help prepare students for post-secondary education in business.

**[Grade 12 International Business Fundamentals (BBB4M)](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/bbb4m)** — This course teaches students the importance of international business and trade for the global economy, as well as influential factors in achieving success in international markets.

**[Grade 12 Business Leadership (BOH4M)](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses/boh4m)** — A vital part of running a successful business is having excellent management or leadership. Throughout the course, students learn how to take on the role of a business leader, manage group dynamics, motivate employees, and deal with workplace stress and conflict. These leadership skills can be used in all aspects of life, making BOH4M an excellent course not just for business majors but for all students.

These are just a few of the courses that Canada e Academy offers that can help prepare you for post-secondary programmes in business, while also giving you incredibly important and transferable skills in finance, leadership, and economics that can help in aspects of your everyday life.

# Train Your Brain: How Online Learning Builds Focus and Endurance

From ancient classrooms to modern online learning, the impact of education on human growth has always captured our attention. While there are certainly academic benefits of going to school, such as learning content knowledge and skills, there are also a variety of cognitive benefits that come with learning.

## Cognitive Endurance Improves with Practice

It is not always easy to stay focused and give your full attention to a task at hand. Recent research suggests that cognitive endurance has become a form of human capital. What this means is that your ability to engage in and sustain effortful mental activity over a continuous stretch of time is a way by which you increase your chances of achieving academic success, both in the classroom and beyond.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is cognitive endurance?**
Cognitive endurance refers to the ability to stay mentally focused and engaged in effortful thinking over an extended period of time. It is like building stamina for your brain. The more you practise focusing on challenging tasks, the longer and more effectively you can sustain your attention.

**How does taking an online course help improve cognitive endurance?**
Online learning encourages self-directed study, time management, and focus. At Canada e Academy, you control your own pace, which allows you to gradually increase the amount of time you spend concentrating on meaningful work, strengthening your mental endurance over time.

**How flexible is the Canada e Academy learning model?**
You can start a course at any time of the year and work at your own pace. Some students complete a course in as little as four weeks, while others take up to 12 months. This flexible pacing helps you manage your workload while building focus and self-discipline.

# Access Ontario's World-Class Curriculum from Anywhere in Canada

You do not need to live in Ontario to earn an Ontario Secondary School Diploma. Canada e Academy serves students in every province and territory, offering flexible online courses that fit alongside your current schooling.

---

## Why Out-of-Province Students Choose Canada e Academy

### OSSD Credits Recognised Nationally

The Ontario Secondary School Diploma is recognised by universities and colleges across Canada. Credits earned through Canada e Academy carry the same weight as those earned at any Ontario public or private school.

### No Relocation Required

Study from home in any province or territory. Our courses are fully online and available 24/7, so you never need to travel to Ontario.

### Flexible Scheduling

Take courses alongside your provincial schooling. There are no set class times — study on your schedule, whether that means evenings, weekends, or school breaks.

### Interactive, Animated Lessons

Our courses go beyond text-based learning. Professional animation, voice narration, and interactive multimedia make each lesson engaging and effective.

### Ontario Certified Teachers

Every course is guided by a teacher certified by the Ontario College of Teachers, providing personalised feedback within 24 hours.

## Common Use Cases

- **University preparation** — Take Ontario university preparation courses (e.g., MHF4U, ENG4U, SCH4U) to strengthen your application to Ontario universities
- **Course availability** — Access courses that may not be offered in your province
- **Mark upgrades** — Retake a course through Canada e Academy to improve your grade
- **OSSD pathway** — Earn a full OSSD alongside or instead of your provincial diploma
- **Acceleration** — Fast-track courses during summer or breaks to get ahead

---

## For Quebec Students

Canada e Academy can serve as a pre-university pathway alternative. Earn your OSSD online and apply to Canadian universities through the OUAC 101 application channel.

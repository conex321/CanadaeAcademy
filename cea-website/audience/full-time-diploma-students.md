# Earn Your Ontario Secondary School Diploma Entirely Online

Canada e Academy offers a complete full-time pathway to the Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD). Whether you are starting fresh, transferring from another school, or completing remaining credits, our guidance team helps you plan and achieve your diploma goals.

---

## What Full-Time Means at Canada e Academy

As a full-time student, you take a complete course load working toward all 30 credits required for the OSSD. Canada e Academy becomes your school of record, maintaining your Ontario Student Record (OSR) and Ontario Student Transcript (OST).

Full-time students receive:

- **Guidance counsellor support** — Diploma pathway planning, course selection, and post-secondary application guidance
- **Student success monitoring** — Our team tracks your engagement and provides outreach and support when needed
- **Transcript services** — Official Ontario Student Transcript maintained and updated as you complete courses
- **OUAC/OCAS integration** — Marks submitted directly to Ontario university and college application centres
- **Report cards** — Mid-course progress reports and final report cards issued for every course

---

## OSSD Requirements

To graduate, you need:

- **30 credits** (compulsory + elective)
- **40 hours of community involvement**
- **Literacy requirement** (OSSLT or OSSLC)
- **2 online learning credits** (all Canada e Academy courses count)

[View detailed OSSD requirements](https://canadaeacademy.com/ossd-requirements)

## Transferring Credits

If you are transferring from another school (Ontario or elsewhere), your existing credits may transfer to Canada e Academy:

- **Ontario students:** Credits already earned at an Ontario school transfer directly.
- **Out-of-province students:** Credits may be evaluated for equivalency.
- **International students:** Through the PLAR process, up to 75% of credits may be transferred.

Contact our guidance team with your transcripts and we will create a personalised diploma plan.

1. **Contact us** — Email info@canadaeacademy.com with your transcripts. Our guidance team assesses your credits and creates a diploma plan.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I really earn my full OSSD online?
Yes. You can complete all 30 credits required for the OSSD through Canada e Academy.

### How long does it take to earn the OSSD?
This depends on how many credits you need. If transferring many credits, it could take as few as several months. Starting from scratch, plan for 2–4 years depending on your pace.

### Do I get a guidance counsellor?
Yes. Full-time diploma students have access to guidance counselling for course selection, diploma planning, and post-secondary applications.

### How do I apply to university?
Canada e Academy submits your marks directly to OUAC (for Ontario universities) and OCAS (for Ontario colleges). You apply through the same 101 channel as all Ontario high school students.

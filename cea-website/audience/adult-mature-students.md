# It Is Never Too Late to Finish What You Started

Whether you are looking to earn your high school diploma, upgrade your marks for post-secondary admission, or take courses for a career change, Canada e Academy offers flexible online learning designed for adult learners.

---

## Do You Qualify as a Mature Student?

You are considered a mature student if:

- You are **not currently enrolled** in a high school, **and**
- You have not attended high school for **at least one year** since turning 18.

---

## Why Canada e Academy for Adult Learners?

### Flexible, Self-Paced Learning

You do not have to put your life on hold to advance your education. Our asynchronous format means you can watch lessons and complete coursework at any time, day or night. No set class times, no rigid deadlines.

### Earn Your OSSD

Mature students who have not received their Ontario Secondary School Diploma can graduate with Canada e Academy upon completion of all OSSD requirements. Our guidance team helps you map out the fastest path to graduation.

### Upgrade Your Marks

Need a higher grade for post-secondary admission? Canada e Academy offers upgrade courses that allow you to retake a course and aim for a better result.

### Pathway Changes

Considering a new career direction? Take the prerequisite courses you need for a different post-secondary programme. Our guidance counsellors can help you identify the right pathway.

### Confidential Records

Your academic records are maintained confidentially. Study on your own terms without disruption to your current employment or personal life.

## How It Works

1. **Contact us** — Email info@canadaeacademy.com with a copy of your academic transcripts and proof of age. We will assess your previously earned credits and help you map out a path to your goal.
2. **Register** — Once your credits are assessed, register for your courses online.
3. **Start learning** — Access your course immediately and study at your own pace.
4. **Earn your credit** — Upon completion, your marks are recorded on the Ontario Student Transcript.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I take courses with Canada e Academy even if I am already a high school graduate?
Yes. Whether you want to broaden your knowledge or need specific credits for post-secondary purposes, online high school courses are a flexible way to advance your education and career prospects.

### I do not have the prerequisite for the course I need. Can you help?
If you are not sure whether you meet prerequisite requirements, email info@canadaeacademy.com with a copy of your academic transcripts and proof of age. We will assess your credits and either pre-approve your prerequisites or help you plan a path to the courses you need.

### Can I graduate through Canada e Academy?
Yes. Mature students who have not received their OSSD can graduate with Canada e Academy upon completion of all OSSD requirements.

### How will my post-secondary institution receive my marks?
Our administration team sends your marks directly to your universities or colleges. All we need is the admissions contact for the institution and the student ID number they issued you when you applied.

### When can I start?
Canada e Academy offers continuous enrolment. Register and begin your studies any day of the year. Access to course content is immediate upon payment.

# Register Online

Enrolment is open year-round. Register today and get immediate access to your course.

---

## How to Register

1. **Browse Courses** — Visit the [course catalog](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses) and select the course you wish to take.
2. **Add to Cart** — Click "Enrol Now" on the course page to add it to your cart.
3. **Complete Your Information** — Fill out the registration form with your personal and academic details.
4. **Submit Payment** — Pay securely online. Payment is processed through our secure payment gateway.
5. **Start Learning** — You will receive a welcome email from your Ontario Certified Teacher and immediate 24/7 access to your course.

## What You Need to Register

### All Students

- Government-issued ID
- Email address
- Payment method (credit card, debit card)

### International Students (Additional)

- Valid passport
- Official academic transcripts (translated to English if necessary)
- PLAR / Equivalent Credit Assessment (if transferring credits — additional fee applies)
- Proof of English proficiency (IELTS, TOEFL, or evidence of English-medium schooling) or enrolment in Canada e Academy's ESL programme
- Prerequisite waiver application (for students without Ontario curriculum background)

[Learn more about international enrolment](https://canadaeacademy.com/international-students)

## Important Information

- **Continuous enrolment:** Register any day of the year and begin immediately.
- **No permission required:** As a private school, Canada e Academy does not require permission from your day school or school board.
- **Course completion window:** You have up to 12 months from your registration date. Many students complete courses in 4–8 weeks.
- **Refund policy:** There are no refunds. Students may transfer to another available course within 7 days of registration, provided they have not attempted any evaluations and have the prerequisite for the new course.

# Maame Crentsil

**Canada e Academy Graduate**

---

Maame Crentsil graduated with an OSSD from Canada e Academy while studying in Accra, Ghana. She was focused on a science pathway, completing Grade 12 Chemistry (SCH4U), Biology (SBI4U), and Physics (SPH4U). She also completed Grade 12 Advanced Functions (MHF4U), Calculus and Vectors (MCV4U), and Grade 12 English (ENG4U). She achieved high scores in all of her subjects.

**Why did you decide to take courses at Canada e Academy?**

I chose Canada e Academy for these courses because it provided me with a flexible schedule that allowed me to carry out my normal activities while completing my school work. The teachers and counsellors are very helpful, the courses are positively challenging while still being understandable and well-organised, the administration and teachers are quick to respond, teachers provide feedback on every piece of work completed with the goal of assisting you in improving yourself, and the school website is simple to navigate. Also, Canada e Academy offers a wide range of courses you can choose from.

**How has Canada e Academy prepared you for post-secondary?**

With each course you take at Canada e Academy, you get a taste of what to expect in post-secondary education. It also taught me how to work independently and manage my time well. I likewise learned how to properly cite sources for every subject, which is a crucial skill to adapt for post-secondary education.

**What were your favourite parts of learning with Canada e Academy?**

The highlights of learning with Canada e Academy, personally, were the wonderful group of polite teachers and counsellors who were always willing to assist with any issues, even if you were located millions of miles away. As a student who completed numerous science courses, I appreciated the virtual labs that brought to life the concepts covered in courses like SBI4U, SCH4U, and SPH4U.

**How did your experience taking courses with Canada e Academy compare to your day school?**

Canada e Academy offers a flexible schedule, the chance to avoid issues like bullying and negative peer pressure, there are no distractions during classes because the courses are taught using videos, and breaks can be taken whenever you like.

**What advice would you share with students looking to take courses online?**

Go for it! This is your opportunity to take more control of your academics and move at a pace that suits you. Most importantly, Canada e Academy is the best choice.

> "Canada e Academy will help you reach your full potential in your pursuit of excellence."

# Amritpal Sandhu

**Canada e Academy Graduate**

---

**Future goals:** After graduation, Amritpal plans to pursue a CFA designation. He has taken the necessary steps at Laurier to ensure success with this goal.

**Scholarships received:** Amritpal received an entrance bursary during his first semester due to having a 90% average upon acceptance.

**How did Canada e Academy help you move closer to your goals?**

"Canada e Academy was essentially responsible for teaching material at a very high level, which resulted in me achieving a 90% overall average. This led to my acceptance at Laurier and receiving the scholarship. The content learned at Canada e Academy also helped me dramatically -- it was relevant content that I used throughout my four years at university."

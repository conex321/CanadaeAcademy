# Tyler Satino

**Canada e Academy Graduate**

---

Tyler is currently in his fourth year at Queen's University studying economics.

**Future goals:** Tyler is in the process of writing his LSATs for next year's law school admission.

He also played football for Queen's University, receiving an athletic scholarship (up to $4,500 annually) while receiving an annual bursary of $2,800.

**Scholarships received:** In his first year, Tyler received a $2,000 admission bursary for his grades in high school.

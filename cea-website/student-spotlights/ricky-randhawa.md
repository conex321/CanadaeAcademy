# Ricky Randhawa

**Canada e Academy Graduate**

---

Ricky is currently at Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly Ryerson) working towards a Bachelor's degree in Business Technology Management.

**Future goals:** Ricky's goal is to become an IT Consultant with a large company, and eventually open his own IT consulting business.

**Scholarships received:** Ricky received a $500 scholarship from York University, $2,000 from Guelph, and $1,000 from Toronto Metropolitan University.

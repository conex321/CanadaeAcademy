# David Cote

**Canada e Academy Graduate**

---

Like many athletes, David decided to come to Canada e Academy so that he could get an education while also pursuing his athletic goals. Graduating in 2023, he received high scores in all of his university-level courses.

**What courses did you take with Canada e Academy?**

English (ENG4U), Advanced Functions (MHF4U), International Business Fundamentals (BBB4M), Earth and Space Science (SES4U), Grade 12 French Immersion (FIF4U), and the Literacy Course (OLC4O).

**Why did you decide to take courses with Canada e Academy?**

- For the flexibility of my busy hockey schedule
- To help me be more resourceful
- The good reviews from other students
- The advantages of having a teacher who is available all day

**How has Canada e Academy prepared you for post-secondary?**

It prepared me to create my own schedule and be more resourceful and disciplined in doing my work alone.

**What were your favourite parts of learning with Canada e Academy?**

Doing my homework and tests whenever I could, by choosing when to do them and also having the privilege of working from anywhere on my computer.

**How did your experience taking courses with Canada e Academy compare to your day school?**

It was an adaptation at the start but it became an advantage when I got used to it. I was able to receive some help during post-school hours and I was able to work anywhere at any time.

**What advice would you share with students looking to take courses online?**

My advice would be to become disciplined with your work and follow a schedule that works for you.

We thank David for answering our questions and wish him the best of luck in his future pursuits!

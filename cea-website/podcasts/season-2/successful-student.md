# Successful Student
**Season 2, Episode 7**

In this episode, Alicia Cuzner and Dwayne Matthews discuss what success is, what a successful student looks like, and how to become a successful student. Listeners will learn about strategies and tools to become a successful student, and the importance of identity. This podcast also includes a Post-Secondary Spotlight on Algonquin College, where Alicia chats with Algonquin College's recruitment officer Erik Franz on what Algonquin has to offer.

# Accommodations vs. Modification
**Season 2, Episode 2**

In this episode, Alicia Cuzner and Dwayne Matthews discuss the difference between accommodation and modification at school. Our hosts unpack what counts as an accommodation, and how we all accommodate ourselves every day. Listen to learn about what a modified curriculum is, and how it can impact your education journey. This episode also includes a Post-Secondary Spotlight on Seneca College, and an interview with the Seneca College Recruitment and Admissions Officer, Natyra Ramadani.

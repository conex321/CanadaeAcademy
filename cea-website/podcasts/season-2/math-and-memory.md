# Math and Memory
**Season 2, Episode 5**

In this episode, Alicia Cuzner and Dwayne Matthews discuss the facts and research behind studying mathematics and how math memorisation works. Listeners will learn about the statistics behind the EQAO testing in Ontario, and debate the pros and cons of standardised testing. Dwayne covers tips on learning and memorisation, and Alicia discusses some findings in prominent math studies conducted on students learning math.

# Behind the Research in Education
**Season 2, Episode 3**

In this episode, Alicia Cuzner and Dwayne Matthews discuss the research behind IEP meeting experiences, optimal learning environments, and inclusive learning. Our hosts debate the significance of a safe space when learning, and whether or not online learning can help create an optimal learning environment. Alicia unpacks what should be expected in an IEP meeting, and how to navigate the IEP process. This episode also includes a Post-Secondary Spotlight on the University of Ottawa, and an interview with the U of O Recruitment and Admissions Officer, Ms. Ling.

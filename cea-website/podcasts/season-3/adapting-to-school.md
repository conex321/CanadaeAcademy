# Adapting to School
**Season 3, Episode 4**

In today's podcast, Ms. Cuzner looks at ways to successfully adapt to education changes, mandatory online classes, and new technology and innovations. Different ways to be a successful student are explored, and three new Tools for Your Toolbelt are discussed. This episode includes a Student Spotlight with international student Catalina, who shares her experiences with adapting to school and advice for future high school students.

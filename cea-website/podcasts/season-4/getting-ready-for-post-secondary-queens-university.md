# Getting Ready for Post-Secondary and Queen's University
**Season 4, Episode 2**

In this episode, podcast host and Canada e Academy teacher Alicia Cuzner discusses strategies that students who are graduating from high school can use when entering post-secondary. Joining her this episode is Professor Jennifer Davis from Queen's University. Since 2010, Dr. Davis has been a Professor with the Faculty of Education and has worked as a student success coordinator for the World Indigenous Studies in Education (WISE) programme. This episode explores the common trends that Dr. Davis has seen when teaching her first-year students, as well as appropriate ways to incorporate AI into learning.

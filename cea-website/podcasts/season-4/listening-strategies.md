# Listening Strategies
**Season 4, Episode 3**

In this episode, Canada e Academy teacher and podcast host Alicia Cuzner discusses the topic of listening and how it is an active process.

This episode explores different ways to optimise your time for effective learning and defines the process of listening to help you better understand why it is important, even when the topic does not initially interest you.

The episode also features a Student Spotlight with recent graduate Alexandra Toupikov, who serves as Canada e Academy's Book Club leader and coordinator. Alexandra shares a variety of strategies for listening in different situations, whether online or in person.

For more learning resources, or to register for high school courses, please visit [canadaeacademy.com](https://canadaeacademy.com).

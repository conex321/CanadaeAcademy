# University and College Pathways

Canada e Academy prepares students for admission to Canada's top universities and colleges. As a private secondary school inspected by the Ontario Ministry of Education (BSID #665804), our Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD) credits are recognized by every publicly funded post-secondary institution in Ontario and across Canada.

## How It Works

- **Ontario universities** -- Canada e Academy submits marks directly to the Ontario Universities' Application Centre (OUAC) on behalf of students.
- **Ontario colleges** -- Canada e Academy submits marks directly to the Ontario College Application Service (OCAS) on behalf of students.
- **Out-of-province institutions** -- Students can request official transcripts to be sent to any Canadian or international institution.

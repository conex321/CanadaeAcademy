# Pathway to Seneca College

## About Seneca College

Seneca College (officially Seneca Polytechnic) is one of Ontario's largest and most comprehensive public colleges, with multiple campuses across the Greater Toronto Area, including the Newnham Campus in North York, the Seneca@York Campus, the King Campus in King City, and the Markham Campus. The college serves more than 30,000 full-time students each year.

Seneca offers a broad range of programs, including bachelor's degrees, diplomas, graduate certificates, and certificates across fields such as business, information technology, engineering technology, health sciences, creative arts and animation, aviation, community services, and hospitality. The college is especially well known for its programs in animation, information technology, accounting, and aviation. Seneca's strong industry partnerships, co-op placements, and applied learning approach prepare graduates for successful careers. The college also maintains extensive university pathway and transfer agreements with institutions across Canada and internationally.

## How Canada e Academy Students Apply

Canada e Academy students applying to Seneca College use the Ontario College Application Service (OCAS):

- **OCAS application:** All applicants to Ontario colleges apply through [ontariocolleges.ca](https://www.ontariocolleges.ca). Canada e Academy submits marks directly to OCAS on behalf of all registered students.
- **Transcript submission:** Marks are transmitted electronically. Students do not need to send paper transcripts for OCAS applications.

### Key Dates

| Milestone | Typical Deadline |

| OCAS application opens | October |

## Recommended Courses

Ontario college programs generally require the OSSD with specific Grade 12 C or U level courses. Below are common requirements for popular Seneca programs:

| Program Area | Recommended Grade 12 Courses |
|---|---|
| Business | ENG4C or ENG4U, MAP4C or MHF4U |
| Computer Programming and Analysis | ENG4C or ENG4U, MHF4U |
| Nursing (BScN collaborative) | SBI4U, SCH4U, MHF4U, ENG4U |
| Animation | ENG4C or ENG4U, portfolio required |
| Accounting and Finance | ENG4C or ENG4U, MAP4C or MHF4U |
| Aviation (Flight) | ENG4C or ENG4U, MHF4U or MAP4C, SPH4U or SPH4C |
| Social Service Worker | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Hospitality and Tourism | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Broadcasting | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Health Sciences | SBI4U or SBI4C, SCH4U or SCH4C, ENG4C or ENG4U |

*Admission requirements vary by program. Always verify current prerequisites on the [Seneca College website](https://www.senecapolytechnic.ca).*

## English Proficiency

Students who complete Ontario curriculum English courses (such as ENG4U or ENG4C) at Canada e Academy may have English proficiency requirements waived for admission purposes. However, policies vary by institution and by program. Students should confirm English proficiency requirements directly with Seneca College's admissions office.

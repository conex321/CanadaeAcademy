# Pathway to McGill University

## About McGill University

McGill University is one of Canada's most prestigious universities, founded in 1821 in Montreal, Quebec. Ranked consistently among the top universities in the world, McGill is renowned for its rigorous academic standards, groundbreaking research, and vibrant bilingual campus culture. The university attracts students from over 150 countries, making it one of the most internationally diverse institutions in North America.

McGill offers more than 300 programs across 11 faculties, including Arts, Science, Engineering, the Desautels Faculty of Management, the Schulich School of Music, Law, Medicine, and Education. With approximately 40,000 students and a historic downtown Montreal campus, McGill provides an academically challenging and culturally rich experience.

## How Canada e Academy Students Apply

McGill University is located in Quebec and does not use the OUAC system. Ontario students apply directly through McGill's online application portal:

- **Direct application:** Canada e Academy students apply directly through the [McGill Admissions portal](https://www.mcgill.ca/applying). Students should request that Canada e Academy send official transcripts directly to McGill.
- **Transcript submission:** Canada e Academy will provide official transcripts and predicted marks upon student request.

### Key Dates

## Recommended Courses

Ontario OSSD students applying to McGill are expected to present six Grade 12 U or M courses, including specific prerequisites for their chosen program:

| Program Area | Recommended Grade 12 Courses |
|---|---|
| Engineering | MHF4U, MCV4U, SCH4U, SPH4U, ENG4U |
| Management (Desautels) | MHF4U, MCV4U, ENG4U |
| Science | MHF4U, MCV4U, plus two of SBI4U / SCH4U / SPH4U, ENG4U |
| Arts | ENG4U plus programme-specific prerequisites |
| Computer Science | MHF4U, MCV4U, ENG4U |
| Music (Schulich) | ENG4U, audition/portfolio required |
| Nursing (Ingram) | SBI4U, SCH4U, MHF4U, ENG4U |

*Admission requirements vary by program. Always verify current prerequisites on the [McGill University admissions website](https://www.mcgill.ca/applying).*

## English Proficiency

Students who complete Ontario curriculum English courses (such as ENG4U) at Canada e Academy may have English proficiency requirements waived for admission purposes. However, policies vary by institution and by program. Students should confirm English proficiency requirements directly with McGill's admissions office.

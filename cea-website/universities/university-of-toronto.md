# Pathway to the University of Toronto

## About the University of Toronto

The University of Toronto (U of T) is Canada's leading research-intensive university and one of the top-ranked institutions in the world. Founded in 1827, U of T spans three campuses -- St. George (downtown Toronto), Mississauga (UTM), and Scarborough (UTSC) -- and offers more than 700 undergraduate and 200 graduate programs across a wide range of disciplines. The university is consistently ranked first in Canada and among the top 25 globally in major international rankings.

U of T is home to world-renowned faculties including the Rotman School of Management, the Faculty of Applied Science and Engineering, the Temerty Faculty of Medicine, and the Faculty of Arts and Science. With a vibrant campus life, extensive co-op and research opportunities, and a network of over 600,000 alumni, U of T provides students with an exceptional educational experience.

## How Canada e Academy Students Apply

Canada e Academy students applying to the University of Toronto follow the standard Ontario application process:

- **OUAC 101 (Ontario secondary school students):** Students currently enrolled in an Ontario secondary school apply through the OUAC 101 application stream. Canada e Academy submits midterm and final marks directly to OUAC on behalf of all registered students.
- **OUAC 105 (all other applicants):** Students who have previously graduated or are studying outside Ontario use the OUAC 105 stream. Official transcripts can be requested from Canada e Academy.

## Recommended Courses

The following Ontario secondary school courses are commonly required or recommended for admission to popular U of T programs:

| Program Area | Recommended Grade 12 Courses |
|---|---|
| Engineering | MHF4U, MCV4U, SCH4U, SPH4U, ENG4U |
| Computer Science | MHF4U, MCV4U, ENG4U |
| Commerce (Rotman) | MHF4U, MCV4U, ENG4U |
| Life Sciences | SBI4U, SCH4U, MHF4U, ENG4U |
| Arts and Science | ENG4U plus programme-specific prerequisites |
| Nursing | SBI4U, SCH4U, MHF4U, ENG4U |
| Architecture | ENG4U, MHF4U, a portfolio submission |

*Admission requirements vary by program and campus. Always verify current prerequisites on the [University of Toronto admissions website](https://www.utoronto.ca/admissions).*

## English Proficiency

Students who complete Ontario curriculum English courses (such as ENG4U) at Canada e Academy may have English proficiency requirements waived for admission purposes. However, policies vary by institution and by program. Students should confirm English proficiency requirements directly with U of T's admissions office.

# Pathway to George Brown College

## About George Brown College

George Brown College is one of Ontario's largest and most diverse public colleges, located in the heart of downtown Toronto. Founded in 1967, the college serves over 30,000 full-time students across multiple campuses, including the Waterfront Campus, St. James Campus, and Casa Loma Campus.

George Brown is particularly well known for its programs in culinary arts, hospitality, health sciences, business, community services, construction and engineering technologies, design, and early childhood education. The college's urban location provides students with direct access to industry employers, clinical placements, and experiential learning opportunities. George Brown also offers several degree programs and collaborative programs with university partners, providing pathways for students who wish to continue their education at the degree level.

## How Canada e Academy Students Apply

Canada e Academy students applying to George Brown College use the Ontario College Application Service (OCAS):

- **OCAS application:** All applicants to Ontario colleges apply through [ontariocolleges.ca](https://www.ontariocolleges.ca). Canada e Academy submits marks directly to OCAS on behalf of all registered students.
- **Transcript submission:** Marks are transmitted electronically. Students do not need to send paper transcripts for OCAS applications.

### Key Dates

| Milestone | Typical Deadline |
|---|---|
| OCAS application opens | October |
| Equal consideration deadline | February 1 |
| Late applications accepted | On a rolling basis (space permitting) |
| Offers of admission | Ongoing from February |

## Recommended Courses

Ontario college programs generally require the OSSD with specific Grade 12 C or U level courses. Below are common requirements for popular George Brown programs:

| Program Area | Recommended Grade 12 Courses |
|---|---|
| Business | ENG4C or ENG4U, MAP4C or MHF4U |
| Nursing (BScN collaborative) | SBI4U, SCH4U, MHF4U, ENG4U |
| Culinary Arts | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Hospitality and Tourism | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Construction Management | ENG4C or ENG4U, MAP4C or MHF4U |
| Early Childhood Education | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Dental Hygiene | SBI4U, SCH4U, ENG4U |
| Design (Graphic, Game, Fashion) | ENG4C or ENG4U, portfolio may be required |
| Computer Programming | ENG4C or ENG4U, MHF4U |

*Admission requirements vary by program. Always verify current prerequisites on the [George Brown College website](https://www.georgebrown.ca).*

## English Proficiency

Students who complete Ontario curriculum English courses (such as ENG4U or ENG4C) at Canada e Academy may have English proficiency requirements waived for admission purposes. However, policies vary by institution and by program. Students should confirm English proficiency requirements directly with George Brown College's admissions office.

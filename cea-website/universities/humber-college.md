# Pathway to Humber College

## About Humber College

Humber College (officially Humber College Institute of Technology and Advanced Learning) is one of the largest and most comprehensive colleges in Ontario, with campuses in Toronto and the surrounding area, including the North Campus in Etobicoke, the Lakeshore Campus, and the Humber International Graduate School. The college serves more than 33,000 full-time students each year.

Humber is known for its breadth of programming, offering bachelor's degrees, diplomas, graduate certificates, and apprenticeship training across a wide range of fields, including business, media and creative arts, health sciences, social and community services, applied technology, hospitality, and skilled trades. The college's strong emphasis on experiential learning, co-op placements, and industry connections ensures that graduates are career-ready. Humber also offers several university pathway and transfer agreements, enabling students to continue their studies at the degree level.

## How Canada e Academy Students Apply

Canada e Academy students applying to Humber College use the Ontario College Application Service (OCAS):

- **OCAS application:** All applicants to Ontario colleges apply through [ontariocolleges.ca](https://www.ontariocolleges.ca). Canada e Academy submits marks directly to OCAS on behalf of all registered students.
- **Transcript submission:** Marks are transmitted electronically. Students do not need to send paper transcripts for OCAS applications.

### Key Dates

| Milestone | Typical Deadline |
|---|---|
| OCAS application opens | October |
| Equal consideration deadline | February 1 |
| Late applications accepted | On a rolling basis (space permitting) |
| Offers of admission | Ongoing from February |

## Recommended Courses

Ontario college programs generally require the OSSD with specific Grade 12 C or U level courses. Below are common requirements for popular Humber programs:

| Program Area | Recommended Grade 12 Courses |
|---|---|
| Business | ENG4C or ENG4U, MAP4C or MHF4U |
| Nursing (BScN collaborative) | SBI4U, SCH4U, MHF4U, ENG4U |
| Film and Television Production | ENG4C or ENG4U, portfolio may be required |
| Computer Programming | ENG4C or ENG4U, MHF4U |
| Paralegal | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Social Service Worker | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Hospitality and Tourism | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Media Communications | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Fitness and Health Promotion | ENG4C or ENG4U, SBI4U or SBI4C |
| Skilled Trades (Electrical, HVAC) | ENG4C or ENG4U, MAP4C or MCR3U |

*Admission requirements vary by program. Always verify current prerequisites on the [Humber College website](https://humber.ca).*

## English Proficiency

Students who complete Ontario curriculum English courses (such as ENG4U or ENG4C) at Canada e Academy may have English proficiency requirements waived for admission purposes. However, policies vary by institution and by program. Students should confirm English proficiency requirements directly with Humber College's admissions office.

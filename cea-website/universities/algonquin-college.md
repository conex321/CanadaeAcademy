# Pathway to Algonquin College

## About Algonquin College

Algonquin College of Applied Arts and Technology serves the National Capital Region and the outlying areas of Eastern Ontario, Western Quebec, and Upstate New York. The college has three campuses, all in Ontario: a primary campus located in Ottawa, and secondary campuses located in Perth and Pembroke.

Algonquin College offers bachelor's degrees, diplomas, graduate certificates, and certificates in a wide range of disciplines and specialties, including business, technology, health sciences, media and design, hospitality, and skilled trades. With a strong emphasis on applied, hands-on learning and industry partnerships, Algonquin prepares students for direct entry into the workforce. The college is one of the largest in Ontario, serving over 40,000 students annually across its full-time, part-time, and online programs.

## How Canada e Academy Students Apply

Canada e Academy students applying to Algonquin College use the Ontario College Application Service (OCAS):

- **OCAS application:** All applicants to Ontario colleges apply through [ontariocolleges.ca](https://www.ontariocolleges.ca). Canada e Academy submits marks directly to OCAS on behalf of all registered students.
- **Transcript submission:** Marks are transmitted electronically. Students do not need to send paper transcripts for OCAS applications.

### Key Dates

| Milestone | Typical Deadline |

## Recommended Courses

Ontario college programs generally require the OSSD with specific Grade 12 C or U level courses. Below are common requirements for popular Algonquin programs:

| Program Area | Recommended Grade 12 Courses |
|---|---|
| Business | ENG4C or ENG4U, MAP4C or MHF4U |
| Computer Programming | ENG4C or ENG4U, MHF4U |
| Nursing (BScN collaborative) | SBI4U, SCH4U, MHF4U, ENG4U |
| Skilled Trades (Electrical, Plumbing) | ENG4C or ENG4U, MAP4C or MCR3U |
| Media and Design | ENG4C or ENG4U, portfolio may be required |
| Hospitality and Tourism | ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Health Sciences | SBI4U or SBI4C, SCH4U or SCH4C, ENG4C or ENG4U |
| Police Foundations | ENG4C or ENG4U |

*Admission requirements vary by program. Always verify current prerequisites on the [Algonquin College website](https://www.algonquincollege.com).*

## English Proficiency

Students who complete Ontario curriculum English courses (such as ENG4U or ENG4C) at Canada e Academy may have English proficiency requirements waived for admission purposes. However, policies vary by institution and by program. Students should confirm English proficiency requirements directly with Algonquin College's admissions office.

# Pathway to Toronto Metropolitan University

## About Toronto Metropolitan University

Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU), formerly known as Ryerson University, is a public research university located in the heart of downtown Toronto, Ontario. Founded in 1948, TMU has evolved into one of Canada's most comprehensive and career-focused universities, with approximately 46,000 students enrolled across a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs.

TMU is especially well regarded for its programs in engineering, business (the Ted Rogers School of Management), media and communications, nursing, architecture, computer science, and creative industries. The university is known for its emphasis on innovation, entrepreneurship, and experiential learning, with strong ties to Toronto's thriving business and technology sectors. TMU's Zone Learning network -- a collection of innovation zones -- gives students real-world experience through start-up incubators and industry partnerships.

## How Canada e Academy Students Apply

Canada e Academy students applying to Toronto Metropolitan University use the OUAC system:

- **OUAC 101:** Students currently enrolled at Canada e Academy apply through the 101 stream. Midterm and final marks are submitted directly by Canada e Academy.
- **OUAC 105:** Students who have previously graduated or are applying from outside Ontario use the 105 stream. Official transcripts can be requested from Canada e Academy.

## English Proficiency

Students who complete Ontario curriculum English courses (such as ENG4U) at Canada e Academy may have English proficiency requirements waived for admission purposes. However, policies vary by institution and by program. Students should confirm English proficiency requirements directly with TMU's admissions office.

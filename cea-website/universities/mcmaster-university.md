# Pathway to McMaster University

## About McMaster University

McMaster University is a world-renowned research university located in Hamilton, Ontario. Founded in 1887, McMaster is consistently ranked among Canada's top universities and is internationally recognized for its innovative, problem-based approach to learning -- particularly in the health sciences. The university's DeGroote School of Medicine pioneered the problem-based learning model now used by medical schools worldwide.

McMaster offers more than 160 programs across six faculties: the DeGroote School of Business, Engineering, Health Sciences, Humanities, Science, and Social Sciences. With approximately 37,000 students, a 121-hectare main campus, and a strong commitment to community-engaged research, McMaster provides a dynamic and supportive learning environment.

## How Canada e Academy Students Apply

Canada e Academy students applying to McMaster University use the Ontario Universities' Application Centre (OUAC):

- **OUAC 101:** Students currently enrolled at Canada e Academy as an Ontario secondary school student apply through the 101 stream. Midterm and final marks are submitted directly by Canada e Academy.
- **OUAC 105:** Students who have previously graduated or are applying from outside Ontario use the 105 stream and can request official transcripts from Canada e Academy.

### Key Dates

## English Proficiency

Students who complete Ontario curriculum English courses (such as ENG4U) at Canada e Academy may have English proficiency requirements waived for admission purposes. However, policies vary by institution and by program. Students should confirm English proficiency requirements directly with McMaster's admissions office.

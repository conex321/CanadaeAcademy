# Admissions

Canada e Academy welcomes students from Ontario, across Canada, and around the world. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and accessible.

---

## Eligibility

Canada e Academy accepts students who are:

- **Ontario students** — Currently enrolled in a day school or not enrolled in any school
- **Out-of-province Canadian students** — Residing in any province or territory
- **International students** — Residing outside Canada
- **Mature/adult students** — Age 18+ who have not attended high school for at least one year
- **Elementary students** — Eligible for the Reach Ahead programme (Grades 7–8)

---

## Enrolment Requirements

### All Students

- Government-issued ID
- Email address
- Payment method

### Students Transferring Credits

- Official academic transcripts from your previous school
- Contact information for your previous school (for OSR transfer)

### International Students

- Valid passport
- Official academic transcripts (translated to English if necessary)
- PLAR assessment (additional fee applies)
- Proof of English proficiency or enrolment in our ESL programme

---

## How to Apply

1. **Browse** the [course catalog](https://canadaeacademy.com/courses)
2. **Register** online at [canadaeacademy.com/register](https://canadaeacademy.com/register)
3. **Submit** required documents to info@canadaeacademy.com
4. **Start** learning immediately upon registration

---

## Continuous Enrolment

Canada e Academy offers continuous enrolment year-round. There are no application deadlines, intake dates, or waiting periods. Register any day and begin your course immediately.

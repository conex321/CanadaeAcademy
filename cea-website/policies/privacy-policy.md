# Privacy Policy

Canada e Academy is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our students, parents, and visitors. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

---

## Information We Collect

When you register for a course or use our website, we may collect:

- **Personal information:** Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, date of birth
- **Academic information:** Transcripts, prior learning records, course enrolment and grades
- **Payment information:** Credit card or payment details (processed securely through our payment gateway)
- **Technical information:** IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies for website functionality

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

- Process course registrations and payments
- Provide access to course materials and the learning platform
- Communicate with you about your courses, grades, and student services
- Maintain your Ontario Student Record (OSR) and Ontario Student Transcript (OST)
- Submit marks to OUAC, OCAS, day schools, and other institutions at your request
- Send newsletters and updates (you may opt out at any time)
- Improve our website and services

---

## How We Protect Your Information

- Payment information is processed securely through encrypted channels and is not stored on our servers
- Access to student records is restricted to authorised staff
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect data

---

## Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

- **OUAC and OCAS** — at your request, for post-secondary applications
- **Your day school** — to transfer report cards and credits
- **Ontario Ministry of Education** — for credit reporting through OnSIS, as required
- **Service providers** — such as payment processors and email services, who assist in operating our school

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal information held by Canada e Academy
- Request corrections to inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information (subject to legal record-keeping requirements)
- Opt out of marketing communications

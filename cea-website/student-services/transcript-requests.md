# Transcript Requests

Canada e Academy maintains your Ontario Student Transcript (OST) and can send your marks to schools, universities, colleges, and employers on your behalf.

---

## What We Can Send

- **Report cards** — Mid-course progress reports and final report cards
- **Ontario Student Transcript (OST)** — Your official OSSD credit record
- **Marks to OUAC** — For Ontario university applications
- **Marks to OCAS** — For Ontario college applications
- **Marks to your day school** — Automatically sent upon course completion
- **Marks to other institutions** — Upon request

---

## How to Request

Contact our student services team with the following details:

- Your full name and student ID
- The institution or person you want the transcript sent to
- The institution's contact email or mailing address
- Your application reference number (if applicable — e.g., OUAC or OCAS number)

**Email:** info@canadaeacademy.com

There is no charge for sending marks to OUAC, OCAS, or your day school.

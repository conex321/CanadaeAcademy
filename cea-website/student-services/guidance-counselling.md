# Guidance Counselling

Canada e Academy's guidance counsellors are here to support you with course selection, diploma planning, and post-secondary preparation. Whether you are a full-time diploma student, an Ontario day school student taking one course, or an international student planning your OSSD pathway, we can help.

---

## What We Help With

- **Course selection** — Choosing the right courses for your goals and prerequisites
- **Diploma planning** — Mapping out all 30 credits required for the OSSD
- **Post-secondary preparation** — Identifying university and college requirements, planning prerequisite courses
- **OUAC/OCAS applications** — Guidance on submitting applications and marks
- **Credit assessment** — Evaluating transcripts from other schools or provinces for credit transfer
- **PLAR (international students)** — Assessing prior learning for international credit transfer
- **Prerequisite waivers** — Evaluating eligibility for prerequisite exemptions

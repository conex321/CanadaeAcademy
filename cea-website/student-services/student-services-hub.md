# Student Services

Canada e Academy is committed to supporting every student's success. Our student services team provides guidance, transcript management, accessibility accommodations, and more.

---

## Our Services

### [Guidance Counselling](https://canadaeacademy.com/student-services/guidance)
Course selection, diploma pathway planning, and post-secondary preparation. Book a free appointment with an experienced guidance counsellor.

### [OUAC Information](https://canadaeacademy.com/student-services/ouac-info)
Information about the Ontario Universities' Application Centre and how Canada e Academy submits your marks for university applications.

### [OCAS Information](https://canadaeacademy.com/student-services/ocas-info)
Information about the Ontario College Application Service and how we submit your marks for college applications.

### [Transcript Requests](https://canadaeacademy.com/student-services/transcript-requests)
Request your Ontario Student Transcript or have your marks sent to a school, employer, or institution.

### [Final Exams](https://canadaeacademy.com/student-services/final-exams)
Information about proctored final evaluations, how to arrange a proctor, and exam scheduling.

### [IEP Accommodations](https://canadaeacademy.com/student-services/iep-accommodations)
Accommodations for students with Individual Education Plans or documented learning needs.

### [Mental Health Resources](https://canadaeacademy.com/student-services/mental-health-resources)
Wellness resources, crisis lines, and support for students managing mental health challenges.

---

## Student Success Team

Our student success team monitors engagement and attendance patterns across all courses. If you fall behind or appear disengaged, we reach out to provide support, check in, and help you get back on track. You are never just a number at Canada e Academy.

# IEP Accommodations

Canada e Academy is committed to supporting all learners. Students with an Individual Education Plan (IEP) or documented learning needs may receive accommodations to help them succeed.

---

## Available Accommodations

Depending on your needs, accommodations may include:

- **Adjusted deadlines** — Extended timelines for assignments and evaluations
- **Alternative assessments** — Modified evaluation formats to suit different learning styles
- **Assistive technologies** — Support for screen readers, text-to-speech, and other tools
- **Simplified language of instruction** — Adjusted language complexity where appropriate
- **Extended time on exams** — Additional time for proctored final evaluations
- **Frequent breaks** — Built-in breaks during assessments
- **Modified presentation** — Adjusted formatting of course materials

---

## How to Request Accommodations

1. **Provide documentation** — Submit your existing IEP, psychoeducational assessment, or medical documentation to info@canadaeacademy.com.
2. **Review** — Our team reviews your documentation and develops an accommodation plan.
3. **Implementation** — Your teachers are informed of your accommodations and apply them throughout your course.

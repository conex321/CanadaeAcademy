# OCAS Information

The Ontario College Application Service (OCAS) processes applications to Ontario's public colleges. As a Canada e Academy student, your marks are submitted directly to OCAS on your behalf.

---

## How It Works

1. **Create your OCAS account** and begin your application at [ontariocolleges.ca](https://www.ontariocolleges.ca).
2. **Provide your OCAS account number** (begins with 2100) when requesting your report card from Canada e Academy.

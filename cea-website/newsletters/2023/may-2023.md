# Canada e Academy Newsletter — May 2023

## Graduation

Though we have just passed the midterm deadlines, senior students already need to start planning for graduation. Many students will be getting their conditional offers over the next month for their post-secondary programmes, but just because you receive an offer does not mean that you can stop focusing on your school work. You will still need to maintain your grades and graduate on time to keep your offers.

# Canada e Academy Newsletter — August 2023

## Hybrid Learning

Hybrid learning has many benefits for students, allowing them the social aspect of attending a physical high school while also getting the flexibility online learning provides. With Canada e Academy, students can take courses part-time with us, while being enrolled full-time in a brick-and-mortar high school.

# Canada e Academy Newsletter — February 2024

As we start heading towards spring, senior students are starting to feel the pressure of completing high school, sending in records to go with their post-secondary applications, and preparing to graduate.

## OUAC Information

The next collection period for OUAC is from March 18th to April 25th. This collection period is for all available final grades and midterm grades. Updated records must be submitted to OUAC by no later than April 25th.

## Adding Canada e Academy to OUAC

If you are a part-time student with Canada e Academy and you need us to send records to OUAC directly, you will need to add Canada e Academy to your OUAC account.

## New Courses

- NBE3U: Grade 11 English Understanding Contemporary First Nations, Metis, and Inuit Voices
- FRA6G: Grade 6 Core French

## Upgrade/Repeat Courses

At Canada e Academy, we want to see students successfully complete courses and be able to pursue their post-secondary goals. With an Upgrade course, you can repeat a course and complete it in as little as 3 weeks.

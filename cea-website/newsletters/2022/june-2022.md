# Canada e Academy Newsletter — June 2022

## OUAC Deadlines

For senior students applying to post-secondary this year, the final dates for OUAC submission are fast approaching. Check your course homepages for the latest deadlines.

## OUAC Admission Information Services

Have you or a friend not received an Ontario university offer? Do not worry -- Canada e Academy can help. Connect with our Guidance Department to get assistance with OUAC Admission Information Services (AIS).

## Preparing for Post-Secondary

Canada e Academy has two free courses that can help students get ready for their post-secondary journey: University Writing Preparation and Supporting Mental Health.

## Summer Courses

With Canada e Academy, summer presents ample opportunities to start off strong in September, without compromising your summer holiday! With great options for students from Grade 8 and onwards, Canada e Academy allows both public and private school students to take advantage of the summer months to supplement their learning with the support of OCT-certified instructors.

## Podcast

Be sure to check out our latest podcast. In this episode, Alicia Cuzner and Dwayne Matthews discuss what an IEP is, and the misconceptions behind IEPs.

# Canada e Academy Newsletter — September 2022

It is that time of year again -- fall has arrived and with it comes the start of a new school year. Perhaps you have gotten your class schedule but are unhappy with the courses you are in? Maybe there was not enough room in a class you really wanted to take or your school does not offer a course that you need? If the answer is yes, Canada e Academy could be the solution for you.

## Full Ontario Curriculum

Canada e Academy offers the full Ontario high school curriculum online. We have courses in every subject, like the main core subjects of Math, English, French, and Science. We also offer several elective subjects such as Art, the Humanities, and Business.

## Full-Time Students

With Canada e Academy, you can sign up as a full-time student and never have to attend a regular day school. You can customise your learning to fit your schedule. As a parent, you can rest assured that your teen is getting a quality education from the comfort of home, with an Ontario Certified teacher attached to each course.

## French Immersion

At Canada e Academy, students are able to take French Immersion courses. We offer FIF courses in Grades 9 through 12, as well as a variety of other subjects in French Immersion such as business, math, science, humanities, history, and geography.

## International Languages

Along with French, there are all kinds of international languages that students can learn, including Farsi (Persian), Hindi, Spanish, Punjabi, Mandarin, and Korean.

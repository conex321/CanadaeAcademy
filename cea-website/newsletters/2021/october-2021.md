# Canada e Academy Newsletter — October 2021

We hope you have all settled into your school routines! October brings with it the season of post-secondary research and applications for senior students!

## Planning Your Path to Post-Secondary

The Ontario University Fair is taking place Tuesday, October 26, from 5-7 pm (ET). All Ontario universities will be attending this free online fair.

## The Application Process

Be sure to check out our OUAC and OCAS Information pages for a current listing of important dates for the 2021-2022 application cycle.

## Canada e Academy x Labster

Canada e Academy is proud to partner with Labster, the world's leading provider of virtual science labs, to bring innovative and interactive virtual lab experiences to each of our 15 science courses, from Grades 9 through 12!

## New Course Spotlight: Communications Technology

Canada e Academy is proud to introduce a new post-secondary pathway, with Communications Technology courses for both Grades 11 and 12!

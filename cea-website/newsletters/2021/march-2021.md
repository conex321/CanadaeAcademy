# Canada e Academy Newsletter — March 2021

## Spring Is Just Around the Corner!

Although March Break may no longer be taking place in March, the third month of the year brings longer days and brighter rays -- and we think that is something to smile about!

## OUAC Midterm Collection

OUAC's third and final collection period for midterms is just around the corner! They will be collecting midterm marks from March 15th through until April 22nd.

## Focusing on Your Mental Health

When it comes to preserving our mental health, talking about it is step number one. This month we outline ways to initiate these conversations, help you understand what you can expect from reaching out, and provide you with a wide array of resources for outreach.

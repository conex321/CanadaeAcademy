# Canada e Academy Newsletter — February 2021

## February Is Black History Month!

To mark this important occasion, we will be celebrating influential figures all month long on our social media channels. Follow us on your favourite feed so you do not miss out!

## OUAC Midterm Collection

Are you applying to university this year? OUAC's second collection period for midterms is happening right now! They will be collecting midterm marks until February 11th. The third (and final) collection period will run from March 15th until April 22nd.

## Focusing on Your Mental Health

This month, we highlight the importance of keeping things simple! When we think of modern society, the analogy of a "rat race" often leaps to mind. More so than in any previous generation, today's teens have more to watch, more to read, and more to do than ever before. To avoid the curse of too much choice, we need to take a big step back.

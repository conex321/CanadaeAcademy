# Canada e Academy Newsletter — December 2021

## Season's Greetings!

Happy holidays to you and yours from Canada e Academy! On behalf of the entire team, we wish you health, growth, and good fortune in the year to come. At Canada e Academy, we believe in the value of positive reflection.

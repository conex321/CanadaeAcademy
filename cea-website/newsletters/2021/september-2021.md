# Canada e Academy Newsletter — September 2021

As families across the province weigh the pros and cons of online versus in-person learning, many continue to seek out alternative options to ensure a safe, reliable return to school.

As a school inspected by the Ontario Ministry of Education with more than a decade of online-only experience, Canada e Academy offers an easy-to-use platform that supports multiple learning styles. Our self-paced, fully online courses alleviate concerns surrounding in-person contact, mask and vaccination requirements, as well as inconvenient or unreliable scheduling.

## Canada e Academy x Labster -- Partners in Educational Innovation

Canada e Academy is proud to partner with Labster, the world's leading provider of virtual science labs, to bring innovative and interactive virtual lab experiences to each of our 15 science courses, from Grades 9 through 12!

## Planning Your Path to Post-Secondary

Be sure to mark your calendar for the virtual Ontario Universities' Fairs (OUFs)! Check out our blog for university fair information for across Canada, UK, and USA.

## New Course Spotlight

Canada e Academy is proud to introduce a new, free non-credit resource course that is designed to help students of all ages prepare for the unique challenge of producing high-quality academic writing in a post-secondary environment.

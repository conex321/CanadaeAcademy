# Canada e Academy Newsletter — April 2021

## OUAC Collection Period

Are you applying to university this year? OUAC's collection period is upon us! This final collection period for midterms will be open until April 22nd. If you have not already, be sure to check your course homepages for the corresponding Canada e Academy deadlines to ensure your midterms are updated in time for universities to view!

## Upgrade Your Marks!

Are you looking to upgrade your mark or understanding in a course to better your chances of post-secondary acceptance? Or perhaps you are a mature student who has decided to take advantage of the extra time at home to pursue a change in career? Whatever your reason, Canada e Academy is here to help you improve your grades, with upgrade options available to be completed in as little as 3 weeks!

## Know Your Teacher: Mr. Lewis

Scott Lewis has been teaching with Canada e Academy since 2014 and has helped more than 900 students earn OSSD credits in that time. Over his years with us, Mr. Lewis has taught courses in English, Economics, Accounting, Law, and Applied Math.

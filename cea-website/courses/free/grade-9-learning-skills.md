# Grade 9 Learning Skills

**Type:** Free Preparatory Course | **Credit:** Non-credit | **Cost:** Free

---

## Course Description

This free preparatory course helps students develop the study habits, time management strategies, and self-regulation skills essential for success in online learning. Students will learn how to set goals, create effective study schedules, manage their workload independently, and stay motivated throughout a course. This course is ideal for students who are new to independent or online learning. This course does not earn an OSSD credit.

---

## What You Will Learn

- Develop effective study habits and note-taking strategies for an online learning environment
- Build time management skills, including creating schedules, setting deadlines, and prioritising tasks
- Practise self-regulation techniques such as goal setting, self-monitoring, and reflective learning
- Learn strategies for staying motivated, managing distractions, and maintaining focus during independent study

# Grade 9 Mental Health

**Type:** Free Preparatory Course | **Credit:** Non-credit | **Cost:** Free

---

## Course Description

This free preparatory course supports students as they transition into high school by providing practical strategies for managing stress, maintaining wellness, and building resilience. Students will explore topics related to mental health awareness, healthy coping mechanisms, and self-care routines. The course is designed to help students feel confident and prepared for the academic and social changes that come with starting high school. This course does not earn an OSSD credit.

---

## What You Will Learn

- Understand the foundations of mental health and well-being, including recognising signs of stress and anxiety
- Develop practical stress management techniques, including mindfulness, breathing exercises, and healthy routines
- Build resilience skills to navigate challenges, setbacks, and transitions with confidence
- Learn where and how to access mental health resources and support services

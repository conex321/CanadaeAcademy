# Free Courses

Canada e Academy offers free preparatory courses to help you build the skills and confidence you need before starting your credit courses. These courses do not earn OSSD credits but are valuable preparation tools.

---

## Available Free Courses

### Grade 9 Math Preparation
Build your foundational math skills before starting MTH1W (Grade 9 Mathematics). Covers key concepts from elementary school that you will need for success in high school math.

### Grade 9 Digital Literacy
Develop the digital skills needed for online learning, including navigating our learning platform, using productivity tools, and managing your digital workspace.

### Grade 9 Learning Skills
Build the study habits, time management, and self-regulation skills that will help you succeed in an online learning environment.

### Grade 9 Mental Health
Learn strategies for managing stress, maintaining wellness, and building resilience as you begin high school.

### Grade 12 University Writing Preparation
Strengthen your academic writing skills in preparation for university-level coursework. Covers essay structure, research skills, and citation formats.

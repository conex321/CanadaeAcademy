# Grade 9 Digital Literacy

**Type:** Free Preparatory Course | **Credit:** Non-credit | **Cost:** Free

---

## Course Description

This free preparatory course equips students with the digital skills needed to succeed in an online learning environment. Students will learn to navigate learning platforms, use productivity tools effectively, and manage their digital workspace. Whether you are new to online learning or want to sharpen your technology skills, this course provides practical, hands-on preparation. This course does not earn an OSSD credit.

---

## What You Will Learn

- Navigate online learning platforms confidently, including submitting assignments and accessing course materials
- Use productivity tools such as word processors, spreadsheets, and presentation software for academic work
- Organise and manage your digital workspace, including file management and cloud storage
- Practise responsible digital citizenship, including online safety, communication etiquette, and privacy awareness

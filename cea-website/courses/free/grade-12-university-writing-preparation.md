# Grade 12 University Writing Preparation

**Type:** Free Preparatory Course | **Credit:** Non-credit | **Cost:** Free

---

## Course Description

This free preparatory course helps Grade 12 students strengthen their academic writing skills in preparation for university-level coursework. Students will develop their ability to structure essays, conduct research, integrate evidence, and use proper citation formats. Whether you are preparing for ENG4U or want to refine your writing before post-secondary studies, this course provides a solid foundation in the conventions of academic writing. This course does not earn an OSSD credit.

---

## What You Will Learn

- Master essay structure, including thesis development, paragraph organisation, and effective introductions and conclusions
- Develop research skills, including evaluating sources, synthesising information, and avoiding plagiarism
- Learn citation formats commonly used in post-secondary institutions, including MLA and APA
- Practise revision and editing techniques to improve clarity, coherence, and academic tone

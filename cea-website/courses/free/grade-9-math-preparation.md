**Type:** Free Preparatory Course | **Credit:** Non-credit | **Cost:** Free

## Course Description

This free preparatory course helps students build the foundational math skills they need before starting MTH1W (Grade 9 Mathematics). Covering key concepts from elementary school, this course is designed to fill gaps in understanding and build confidence with the essential skills that form the basis of high school mathematics. This course does not earn an OSSD credit.

---

## What You Will Learn

- Review and strengthen core arithmetic skills, including operations with fractions, decimals, and integers
- Develop fluency with ratios, proportions, and percentages
- Build foundational understanding of algebraic thinking, including variables, expressions, and equations
- Practise problem-solving strategies and mathematical reasoning used in Grade 9 Mathematics

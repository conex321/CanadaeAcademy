# ICD2O: Digital Literacy and Innovation

**Grade:** 10 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** Open
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course helps students develop digital literacy skills as they explore emerging and evolving technologies. Students will explore a variety of computer applications and develop a deeper understanding of the nature of technology, digital environments, and the impact of digital technologies on individuals and society. Students will learn to use technology safely, responsibly, and effectively, and to develop transferable digital skills.

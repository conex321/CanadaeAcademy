# TEJ2O: Computer Technology

**Grade:** 10 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** Open
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course introduces students to computer systems, networking, and interfacing, as well as electronics and robotics. Students will assemble, repair, and configure computers and small networks. They will also build electronic circuits and simple robotic systems. Students will develop an awareness of environmental and societal issues related to the use of computers and electronic technology, and will learn about secondary and post-secondary pathways leading to careers in computer technology.

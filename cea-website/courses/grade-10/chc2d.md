# CHC2D: Canadian History Since World War I

**Grade:** 10 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** Academic
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course explores the impact of various social, economic, and political developments and events on the lives of different Canadian groups since 1914. Students examine the role of conflict and cooperation in Canadian society, Canada's evolving role within the global community, and the impact of various individuals, organisations, and events on Canadian identity, citizenship, and heritage. Students develop their ability to interpret and analyse evidence through the investigation of key issues and events in Canadian history from the past century.

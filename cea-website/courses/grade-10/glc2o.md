# GLC2O: Career Studies

**Grade:** 10 | **Credit Value:** 0.5 | **Course Type:** Open
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course encourages students to develop the skills, knowledge, and habits that will allow them to be successful in life after graduation. Students make connections between their skills, interests, and values and the array of post-secondary options available to them. They also learn about personal financial management.

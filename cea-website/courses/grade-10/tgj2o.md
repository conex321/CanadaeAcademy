# TGJ2O: Communications Technology

**Grade:** 10 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** Open
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course introduces students to communications technology from a media perspective. Students will work in the areas of TV/video and movie production, radio and audio production, print and graphic communications, photography, and animation. Student work will include projects that reflect both their practical skills and their understanding of the theoretical aspects of the course material.

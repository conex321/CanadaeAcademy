# ENG2P: English

**Grade:** 10 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** Applied
**Prerequisite:** ENG1D or ENG1P or ENL1W

---

## Course Description

This course extends students' skills in oral communication, reading, writing, and media literacy. Students study and interpret a variety of informational, literary, and graphic texts. An important focus of the course is on consolidating students' strategies for developing effective communication and on preparing students for success in the Grade 11 English workplace preparation or college preparation course.

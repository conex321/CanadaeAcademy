# MAP4C: Foundations for College Mathematics

**Grade:** 12 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** College Preparation
**Prerequisite:** MBF3C, Grade 11 Foundations for College Mathematics or MCF3M, Grade 11 Functions and Applications or MCR3U, Grade 11 Functions

---

## Course Description

This course enables students to broaden their understanding of real-world applications of mathematics. Students analyse data using statistical methods; solve problems involving applications of geometry and trigonometry; solve financial problems connected with annuities, budgets, and renting or owning accommodation; simplify expressions; and solve equations. Students reason mathematically and communicate their thinking as they solve multi-step problems. This course prepares students for college programmes in areas such as business, health sciences, and human services, and for certain skilled trades.

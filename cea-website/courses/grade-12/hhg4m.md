# HHG4M: Human Growth and Development

**Grade:** 12 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** University/College Preparation
**Prerequisite:** Any Grade 11 or 12 university (U) or university/college (M) preparation course in Social Sciences and Humanities, English, or Canadian and World Studies

---

## Course Description

This course offers a multidisciplinary approach to the study of human development throughout the life cycle. Students examine how early brain and childhood development are linked to lifelong learning, health, and well-being. They develop child-care and human-relationship skills through lessons and activities with practical applications, exploring topics such as development theories, physical development, cognitive development, and socio-emotional development.

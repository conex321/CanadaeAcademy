# SBI4U: Biology

**Grade:** 12 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** University Preparation
**Prerequisite:** SBI3U, Grade 11 Biology

---

## Course Description

This course provides students with the opportunity for in-depth study of the concepts and processes that occur in biological systems. Students study theory and conduct investigations in the areas of biochemistry, metabolic processes, molecular genetics, homeostasis, and population dynamics. Emphasis is placed on the achievement of detailed knowledge and the refinement of skills needed for further study in various branches of the life sciences and related fields.

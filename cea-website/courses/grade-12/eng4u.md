# ENG4U: English

**Grade:** 12 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** University Preparation
**Prerequisite:** ENG3U, Grade 11 English or NBE3U, English: Understanding Contemporary First Nations, Metis, and Inuit Voices

---

## Course Description

This course consolidates the literacy, communication, creative, and critical thinking skills necessary for success in both academic and daily life. Students analyse a range of challenging literary, informational, and graphic texts from various periods, countries, and cultures. They also create oral, written, and media texts in a variety of forms. The curriculum focuses on clear and confident use of academic language, purposeful reading and reading strategy, and developing greater control in writing.

# MAT7G: Mathematics

**Grade:** 7 | **Course Type:** Middle School
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course builds on students' existing mathematical knowledge and introduces key concepts in number sense and numeration, algebra, data management, geometry, measurement, and financial literacy. Through interactive, animated lessons, you will develop your ability to think critically and solve problems in real-world contexts. You will explore proportional reasoning, develop fluency with operations on fractions and integers, and begin working with algebraic expressions and equations. Activities are designed to strengthen your confidence in mathematics and prepare you for success in Grade 8 and beyond.

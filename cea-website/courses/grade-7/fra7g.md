# FRA7G: Core French

**Grade:** 7 | **Course Type:** Middle School
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course builds your French-language skills through listening, speaking, reading, and writing activities. You will expand your vocabulary, develop your understanding of writing conventions, and explore aspects of French-speaking communities in Canada and around the world. Interactive animated lessons guide you through real-life scenarios — ordering food, describing your community, discussing interests — to make language learning engaging and practical. By the end of this course, you will be able to communicate with greater confidence in everyday situations and will have a stronger appreciation for Francophone culture.

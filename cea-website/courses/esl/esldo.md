# ESLDO: English as a Second Language, Level 4

**Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** Open
**Prerequisite:** ESLCO

---

## Course Description

This course prepares students to use English with increasing fluency and accuracy in both academic and social settings and to participate in Canadian society as informed citizens. Students develop the oral-presentation, reading, and writing skills required for success in all areas of school and life.

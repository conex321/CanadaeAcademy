# HFC3M: Food and Culture

**Grade:** 11 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** University/College Preparation
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course focuses on the foods, flavours, cooking techniques, and cultural traditions of global cuisines. Students will develop practical cooking and food-related etiquette skills as they explore the origins and development of diverse food traditions, examining how Canadian food choices and traditions have been influenced by other cultures.

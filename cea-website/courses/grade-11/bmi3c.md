# BMI3C: Marketing

**Grade:** 11 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** College Preparation
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course introduces the fundamental concepts of product marketing and examines how trends, issues, global economic changes, and technology influence consumer buying habits. Students will engage in market research, develop marketing strategies, and produce a marketing plan for a product of their choice. Key topics include the 4 Ps of marketing (product, price, place, and promotion), product development, distribution strategies, and the influence of new information technologies on marketing.

# BDI3C: Introduction to Entrepreneurship

**Grade:** 11 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** College Preparation
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course focuses on how entrepreneurs recognise opportunities, generate ideas, and organise resources to plan successful ventures and achieve their goals. Through hands-on experiences, students will have opportunities to develop the values, traits, and skills most often associated with successful entrepreneurs. Students will analyse the characteristics and contributions of enterprising people, compare various entrepreneurs, and assess their own entrepreneurial potential.

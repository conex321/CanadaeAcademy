# SCH3UF: Chimie (French Immersion)

**Grade:** 11 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** University Preparation
**Prerequisite:** SNC2D, Grade 10 Science

---

## Course Description

Ce cours porte sur les concepts et les theories qui forment la base de la chimie moderne. L'eleve etudie les proprietes chimiques de composes solides, liquides et gazeux, examine les reactions et les rapports quantitatifs entre ceux-ci, et evalue les impacts sociaux et environnementaux resultant de la fabrication de nouveaux produits et de l'application de nouveaux processus chimiques.

This course focuses on the concepts and theories that form the basis of modern chemistry. Students study the chemical properties of solid, liquid, and gaseous compounds, examine the reactions and quantitative relationships between them, and evaluate the social and environmental impacts resulting from the manufacture of new products and the application of new chemical processes.

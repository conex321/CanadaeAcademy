# SNC8G: Science

**Grade:** 8 | **Course Type:** Middle School
**Prerequisite:** SNC7G recommended

---

## Course Description

This course builds on your Grade 7 Science knowledge and explores four key areas: Life Systems, Structures and Mechanisms, Matter and Energy, and Earth and Space Systems. You will investigate cells as the basic unit of life, analyse how systems interact in organisms and the environment, examine the properties and behaviour of fluids, and study water systems on Earth. Through interactive animated lessons and virtual investigations, you will strengthen your skills in scientific inquiry, experimental design, and evidence-based reasoning. This course prepares you for success in Grade 9 Science.

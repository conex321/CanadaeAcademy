# MAT8G: Mathematics

**Grade:** 8 | **Course Type:** Middle School
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course strengthens your mathematical reasoning and prepares you for the transition to high school. You will deepen your understanding of number sense and numeration, with a focus on operations with fractions, integers, and proportional relationships. Algebra units introduce you to solving multi-step equations and analysing linear relationships. You will also explore data management and probability, geometric properties and transformations, measurement concepts, and financial literacy. Through interactive animated lessons, you will apply mathematical thinking to real-world problems and develop the confidence you need for Grade 9 Mathematics.

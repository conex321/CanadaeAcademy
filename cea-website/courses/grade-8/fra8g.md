# FRA8G: Core French

**Grade:** 8 | **Course Type:** Middle School
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course builds on your French-language skills and prepares you for Grade 9 Core French. You will develop greater fluency in listening, speaking, reading, and writing through interactive animated lessons set in real-world contexts. Activities focus on expanding your vocabulary, strengthening your command of grammar and sentence structure, and deepening your understanding of French-speaking communities and cultures across Canada and around the world. By the end of this course, you will be able to engage in longer conversations, read and respond to authentic French texts, and write with greater accuracy and confidence.

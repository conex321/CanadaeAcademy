# SCH3U Upgrade: Chemistry

**Grade:** 11 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** University Preparation
**Prerequisite:** Previous completion of SCH3U

---

## Why Upgrade?

Already completed SCH3U but want a higher mark? Canada e Academy allows you to retake the full course with fresh assessments. Your new mark can support a stronger post-secondary application.

- Retake the full course with new assessments
- The higher mark supports your university or college application
- Both attempts appear on the Ontario Student Transcript; in Grades 11 and 12, an "R" is placed next to the lower mark
- Only one attempt earns credit toward the 30 OSSD credits

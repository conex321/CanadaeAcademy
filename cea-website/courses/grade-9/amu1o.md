# AMU1O: Music

**Grade:** 9 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** Open
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course emphasises the creation and performance of music at a level consistent with previous experience. Students develop musical literacy skills by learning the elements of music through performing, creating, and listening to music. Students maintain a portfolio of their work and participate in reflective analysis of their own performances and creations, as well as the music of others.

# GLS1O: Learning Strategies 1: Skills for Success in Secondary School

**Grade:** 9 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** Open
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This course focuses on learning strategies to help students become better, more independent learners. Students learn how to develop and apply literacy and numeracy skills, personal management skills, and interpersonal and teamwork skills to improve their learning and achievement in school and in the community. The course also helps students build confidence and motivation to pursue opportunities for success in secondary school and beyond.

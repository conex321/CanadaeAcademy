# TAS1O: Technology and the Skilled Trades

**Grade:** 9 | **Credit Value:** 1.0 | **Course Type:** Open
**Prerequisite:** None

---

## Course Description

This hands-on course is designed to provide students with opportunities to further explore the engineering design process and develop technological knowledge and skills introduced in earlier grades. Students engage with the engineering design cycle, develop foundational skills in a variety of skilled trades, and apply their learning through practical activities in construction, automotive, hospitality and tourism, and communications technology. Students problem-solve, create, and reflect as they discover how technology shapes our world and their own potential in it.

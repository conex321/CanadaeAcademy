# French Immersion Courses

Canada e Academy offers French as a Second Language courses for students who want to develop or continue their French language skills while earning OSSD credits.

---

## Available French Courses

| Code | Course Name | Grade | Type |
|------|------------|-------|------|
| FSF1D | Core French, Academic | Grade 9 | Academic |
| FSF1O | Core French, Open | Grade 9 | Open |
| FSF2D | Core French | Grade 10 | Academic |

---

## French as a Compulsory Credit

One credit in French as a Second Language is required for the OSSD. Canada e Academy's French courses fulfil this requirement.

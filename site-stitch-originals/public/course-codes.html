Course Pathways Explained

The destination of the course dictates how the material is taught and evaluated.

U

University Preparation

Designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills they need to meet the entrance requirements for university programs.

C

College Preparation

Focused on preparing students for college-level programs and specific career tracks in the technology or service sectors.

M

Mixed (Univ./College)

Includes content relevant for both university and college entrance. Versatile for students exploring multiple post-secondary options.

O

Open

Appropriate for all students, regardless of their post-secondary plans. Often used for elective courses like Phys. Ed or Arts.

E

Workplace Preparation

Prepares students for direct entry into the workplace or for apprenticeship and other training programs.

W

De-streamed

Modern Grade 9 courses that provide a common learning experience for all students to ensure equitable outcomes.

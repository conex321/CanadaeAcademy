About Us

Is Canada e Academy accredited by the Ontario Ministry of Education? expand_more
Yes. Canada e Academy is a fully inspected private secondary school by the Ontario Ministry of Education. Our Ministry BSID number is #665804. We have been providing quality online education since 2010, and all our courses are developed and taught by OCT (Ontario College of Teachers) certified educators.
Who are the teachers at Canada e Academy? expand_more
Every single one of our teachers is a qualified Ontario certified teacher (OCT). They bring years of experience in both traditional and digital classrooms to ensure students receive high-quality feedback and support throughout their learning journey.

What is your refund policy? expand_more
Due to the nature of digital course access and administrative costs, Canada e Academy maintains a strict no-refund policy. Once registration is complete and course materials are accessed, fees are non-refundable. We encourage students to review course prerequisites carefully before enrolling.
How long do I have to complete a course? expand_more
Students have up to 12 months to complete a course from the date of enrolment. However, we offer a fast-track option where highly motivated students can complete a credit in as little as 4 weeks.

Course Experience

Are the courses self-paced or scheduled? expand_more
All courses at Canada e Academy are 100% online and self-paced. This means you can log in 24/7 and work through the material at a speed that suits your schedule. You can start a course any day of the year.
How are final exams proctored? expand_more
Final exams are conducted through an online proctoring service or by an approved local proctor. This ensures the integrity of the OSSD credit and allows you to write your exam safely and securely from home.

OSSD & Graduation

To earn the Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD), a student must earn 30 credits (18 compulsory and 12 elective), complete 40 hours of community involvement, and pass the Literacy requirement (OSSLT).

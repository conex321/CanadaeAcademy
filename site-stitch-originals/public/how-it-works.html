Your Path to an Ontario High School Diploma

Canada e Academy provides a flexible, high-quality online learning environment. Our structured six-step process ensures you earn OSSD credits recognized by universities worldwide, all at your own pace.

04

Submit Assessments

Weight: 70% of Final Grade

Complete assignments and unit tests through the portal. Our Ontario Certified Teachers provide comprehensive feedback and grading within 24-48 business hours, ensuring you stay on track with your goals.

speed

Fast Feedback Loop: Personalized critiques to help you improve before the next assessment.

05

Final Evaluation

Weight: 30% of Final Grade

All Ontario credit courses require a final evaluation. We make this secure and accessible wherever you are located.

security

Proctoring

Final exams must be supervised by an approved proctor (teacher, librarian, or professional) to maintain academic integrity.

calendar_month

Flexible Scheduling

Book your exam date at least 5 days in advance once you have completed all previous assessments in the course.

public

Remote Options

For students abroad, we offer online proctoring solutions through verified platforms to ensure global access.

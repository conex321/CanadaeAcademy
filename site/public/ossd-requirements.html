edit

Literacy Graduation

To earn the OSSD, students must meet the provincial literacy requirement. Most students do this by passing the Ontario Secondary School Literacy Test (OSSLT).

The OLC4O Course

Students who are unsuccessful on the OSSLT twice are eligible to take the Ontario Secondary School Literacy Course (OLC4O) to fulfill this requirement.

devices

Online Learning

The Ministry of Education requires students to earn two online learning credits. Canada e Academy courses are fully asynchronous and count directly towards this requirement.

Opt-Out Available

Families may choose to opt out of the mandatory online learning credits by submitting an Opt-Out Form to the Guidance department.

groups

Community Service

Students must complete a minimum of 40 hours of community involvement activities. This encourages students to develop an awareness of civic responsibility.

Eligible Activities

Activities must be completed outside of scheduled class time and cannot be for-profit work. Consult our handbook for a full list.

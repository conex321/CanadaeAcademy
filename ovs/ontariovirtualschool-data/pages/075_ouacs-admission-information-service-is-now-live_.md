# OUAC's Admission Information Service is Now Live! - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ouacs-admission-information-service-is-now-live
**Description:** OUAC's AIS allows last-minute applicants to search for university programs across Ontario with spaces available for Fall 2021.

---

![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1.jpg)

Haven’t received an offer of admission? OUAC’s [Admission Information Service (AIS)](https://www.ouac.on.ca/apply/referral/en_CA/referral/index) is here to help! Click the link to search a comprehensive list of programs across all Ontario universities that still have spaces available! It’s not too late to pursue your post-secondary goals.

## Course Description For University Writing Preparation Online Course

This course enables students to further develop their writing skills through various activities centered around the types of writing they will encounter in their post-secondary studies. This course is free and will be beneficial to grade 12 students who are wanting to improve and refine their written communication skills.

Unit 1: Essay Writing
Unit 2: Research Paper
Unit 3: University Assignments
Unit 4: Workplace Writing

#### This teacher-supported course is absolutely free! You can register and start anytime.

Please note: completion of this course will NOT earn you a high school credit. This means that completion of this course will not be included on any current or future high school record or transcript. This course is to be considered as a general interest course only.

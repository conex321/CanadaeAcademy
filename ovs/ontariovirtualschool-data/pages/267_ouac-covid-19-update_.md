# OUAC COVID-19 Update - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ouac-covid-19-update
**Description:** As of April 3rd, OUAC has not announced any changes to their midterm collection deadlines. Please continue to follow the dates outlined on your course homepages.

---

![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1.jpg)

As of April 3rd, OUAC has not announced any changes to their midterm collection deadlines. We are monitoring their response to the evolving situation. If an announcement is made regarding extensions, we will adjust our timelines respectively. In the meantime, please continue to follow the dates outlined on your course homepages.

For the latest updates from OUAC, please consult [their website](https://www.ouac.on.ca/news/important-covid-19-update/).

# OUAC Midterm Collections - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ouac-midterm-collections
**Description:** OUAC's next midterm collection period runs from March 16th to April 23rd. April 7th is the registration cutoff for students who need to meet this deadline.

---

![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1.jpg)

OUAC will collect all remaining midterms for 101 Applicants between March 16th and April 23rd.

Tuesday, April 7th will be the registration cutoff for students who need to meet this deadline.

Students are encouraged to sign up well in advance of this date to ensure they have maximum time to complete their assignments.

In order to qualify for a midterm, students must:
– have been enrolled in a course for at least 14 days
– have a valid prerequisite on file (i.e. final report card, transcript, or credit counselling summary)
– have the first 2-3 units worth of assignments in their course completed & marked by their instructor

If you have questions about OUAC, please contact our office or check out our [blog post](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ovs-blog/demystifying-the-world-of-ouac/)!

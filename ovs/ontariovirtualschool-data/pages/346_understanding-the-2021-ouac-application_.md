# OUAC's 2021 Tutorial Videos - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/understanding-the-2021-ouac-application
**Description:** Click through for easy-to-follow tutorials taking you through the application process, including how to ensure your OVS courses are listed!

---

![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1.jpg)

The OUAC has created [6 easy-to-follow tutorials](https://ouac.on.ca/101-tutorials/) covering every step of their application process!

For applicants who are also taking a course (or multiple) through OVS, please pay close attention to [Tutorial #3 between 1:45-2:10](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jI7w92_EBzQ) for a step-by-step run-through of how to add OVS to your profile as an alternate school!

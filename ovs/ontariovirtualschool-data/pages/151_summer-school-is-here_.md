# Summer School is Here! - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/summer-school-is-here
**Description:** Make the most of your summer with OVS! Complete any of our courses in just 3 weeks!

---

![Ontario Virtual School Summer School](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Summer-School-2020-1-500x332.jpg)

Register between May 15th and August 15th to be eligible for our summer fast-track completion – complete ANY course in as little as 3 weeks!

For students looking to repeat a course taken this past semester – either to improve your mark or just your understanding – we are offering a special 2-week upgrade option for ANY of our courses (not just Grade 12!).
Register for any course you’ve taken previously that was negatively impacted by COVID closures and mention “Summer Upgrade” in the notes section. Sign up before July 1st using the code “upgrade2020” to
Check out [our blog post](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ovs-blog/strengthen-your-learning-with-ovs-this-summer/) or [our summer school info page](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/online-summer-school-courses/) for all the details!

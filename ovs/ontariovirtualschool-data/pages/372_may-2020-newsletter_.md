*Spring is in the air!*

We may still be cooped up inside, but that doesn’t mean we can’t throw open the windows and let the breeze revive our spirits! And while we’re staying home & staying safe this month, it’s important to stay connected with each other and the world outside. So…

![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/SOCIAL-300x300.png)

LET’S GET SOCIAL!

Be sure to follow OVS on all your favourite platforms!

[FOLLOW US ON FACEBOOK](https://www.facebook.com/ontariovirtualschool/) | [TWEET US](https://twitter.com/OVSonline)  | [CATCH US ON THE ‘GRAM](https://www.instagram.com/ontariovirtualschool/)

We post weekly blogs, deadline info, study tips, discount codes, plus lots of fun giveaways!

PLUS, be sure to check out [our blog](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ovs-news-blog/) every Monday this month for the latest installment of our “Coping with COVID” Series with Robert Ford (Marketing Director for OVS). He’ll be sharing tips for parents & educators as we continue adjusting to evolving learn from home protocols.

![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Scott-Sanders-1-250x300.png)

# Expanded Immersion Offerings - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/expanded-immersion-offerings
**Description:** OVS is proud to offer an ever-expanding selection of French Immersion, click here to see our current offerings!

---

![immersion courses](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/new-FIF-course-offerings-500x500.jpg)

In addition to the core French Immersion language courses for Grades 9 through 12 ([FIF1D](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/fif1d/), [FIF2D](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/fif2d/), [FIF3U](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/fif3u/), and [FIF4U](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/fif4u/)), OVS is proud to offer additional elective options taught entirely in French.

[BBB4MF, Grade 12 French International Business Fundamentals](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/bbb4mf/)
[BTT1F, Grade 9 French Information and Communication Technology in Business](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/btt1f/)
[BTT2F, Grade 10 French Information and Communication Technolog](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/btt2f/)y
[CGC1F, Grade 9 French Issues in Canadian Geography](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/cgc1f/)
[HFA4UF, Grade 12 French Nutrition and Health](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/hfa4uf/)

Like our English course offerings, all of our French courses are designed and taught by OCT-certified instructors with Native fluency in French. With video and voice narration and interactive multimedia lessons, our French programming offers a flexible opportunity for existing Immersion-stream students to pursue [their French Immersion certification](https://www.tdsb.on.ca/Elementary-School/School-Choices/French-Programs/Secondary-Programs).

For more information on our FIF program, check out [our blog](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/earn-a-bilingual-education-online-through-ovs/) or our French Immersion [course listing](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/french-immersion/)!

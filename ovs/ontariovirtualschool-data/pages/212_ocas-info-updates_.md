### What is OCAS?

“OCAS” stands for Ontario College Application Service.

### What does OCAS do?

[The OCAS is an application processing centre for undergraduate admissions to Ontario Colleges](https://www.ontariocolleges.ca/en). The OCAS receives student applications, processes them and transfers the information to the colleges. The OCAS does not make admission decisions; that is the colleges’ responsibility.

### Will my marks be sent directly to OUAC or OCAS?

Yes, Ontario Virtual School (OVS) will send your marks directly to OCAS.

### How do I apply to OCAS?

Simple, just go to ontariocolleges.ca to apply. [This video](https://www.ocas.ca/support/high-school-partners/how-to-apply-video-guidance-counsellors) will take you through the process.

### How do I access my OCAS account?

You can access your OCAS account by following [this link](https://www.ontariocolleges.ca/en/apply-now) to the OCAS login page.

### When should I apply

As long as space remains in an Ontario college program, you may apply. However, there are some important dates to keep in mind, especially for popular programs that fill up quickly. See below for a list of the current year’s important dates.

### Important Dates and Deadlines for Ontario College Applications (2025-2026)

# Important Dates – Fall 2026 Programs

### Apply

#### Early October, 2025

The online application is available at [ontariocolleges.ca.](http://ontariocolleges.ca.)

#### February 1, 2026

Apply on or before this date in order to receive equal consideration for admission to programs starting the following fall. After this date, colleges consider applicants on a first-come, first-  served basis until programs are full.

### Offer

#### November 1, 2025

Earliest date the colleges may release offers to applicants. Offers will continue to be issued until programs are filled or wait lists are established.

*NOTE:* Applicants who have not met admission requirements for a program may be notified at any time during the application process.

### Accept

#### November 1, 2025

Earliest date that applicants may confirm an offer of admission.

#### May 1, 2026

Applicants must accept their offer of admission by this date. Log into your account to [accept your offer](https://www.ontariocolleges.ca/en/applying/offers-of-admission).

Colleges may extend offers of admission after this date. Each college will set the deadline to accept offers made after May 1. This deadline will be communicated to you with your offer.

#### June 1, 2026

Earliest date that colleges may require payment of tuition fees.

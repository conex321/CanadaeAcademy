# Celebrating Success: Eric Manikhouth Awarded OVS University Acceptance Scholarship - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/celebrating-success-eric-manikhouth-awarded-ovs-university-acceptance-scholarship
**Description:** Ontario Virtual School (OVS) is proud to announce Eric Manikhouth as this year’s recipient of the March 2025 OVS University Acceptance $500 Scholarship! We are thrilled to celebrate Eric’s acceptance into the Computer Science program at the University of Ottawa, where he will continue his academic journey and pursue his passion for technology and innovation.

---

![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Eric-Scholarship-Blog-500x278.png)
[Ontario Virtual School](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/) (OVS) is proud to announce **Eric Manikhouth** as this year’s recipient of the March 2025 **OVS University Acceptance $500 Scholarship**! We are thrilled to celebrate Eric’s acceptance into the [**Computer Science program at the University of Ottawa**,](https://www.uottawa.ca/faculty-engineering/undergraduate-studies/programs/computer-science) where he will continue his academic journey and pursue his passion for technology and innovation.

![University of Ottawa](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/uottawa_hor_black-500x134.png)

Eric’s story is a shining example of how dedication, discipline, and the right learning environment can make a real difference in a student’s success. In a heartfelt social media post, Eric shared how OVS played a significant role in helping him achieve his goals:

***“By offering a flexible, customized, and excellent learning environment, Ontario Virtual School (OVS) was super helpful for me to reach my academic goals. OVS let me study at my own speed as an online platform, which helped me especially balance my studies with other commitments. While the skilled and encouraging teachers gave clear instruction and timely feedback, guaranteeing I completely understood the content, the vast choice of courses offered allowed me to study subjects that matched my interests and future objectives. Furthermore, the availability of tools and the possibility to review courses helped strengthen my knowledge and raise my performance. OVS’s focus on self-discipline and independent learning also helped me to be ready for the challenges of university by preparing me with the tools required to succeed in my university career. All things considered, OVS was crucial in allowing me to reach my academic potential and realize my goals.”***

![Eric Manikhouth](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Eric-Photo-300x605.jpg)

At OVS, we’re honored to have been a part of Eric’s academic journey. His success reflects the values we strive to uphold every day—flexibility, personalized learning, and academic excellence. Eric’s dedication to his studies and his thoughtful reflections on the OVS experience embody the very spirit of what this scholarship represents.

Congratulations once again, Eric! We wish you the very best at the University of Ottawa and beyond. Your journey is just beginning, and we know you’re destined for great things!

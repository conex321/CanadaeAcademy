# Celebrating Success: Alaina Noormohamed Awarded OVS University Acceptance Scholarship - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/celebrating-success-alaina-noormohamed-awarded-ovs-university-acceptance-scholarship
**Description:** Ontario Virtual School (OVS) is proud to announce Alaina Noormohamed as this year’s recipient of the April 2025 OVS University Acceptance $500 Scholarship! We are thrilled to celebrate Alaina’s acceptance into the Social Science program at McMaster.

---

![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Alaina-Noormohamed-Celebrating-Success-500x278.png)

[Ontario Virtual School](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/) (OVS) is proud to announce **Alaina Noormohamed** as this year’s recipient of the April 2025 **OVS University Acceptance $500 Scholarship**! We are thrilled to celebrate Alaina’s acceptance into the [**Social Science** program at McMaster.](https://socialsciences.mcmaster.ca/)

Alaina achieved high scores in her courses with OVS, and we are very proud to have been a small part of her journey.

![Alaina Noormohamed Scholarship](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Alaina-scaled.jpg)

***The instructors at OVS were knowledgeable and responsive, providing clear explanations and timely, constructive feedback that helped me master course content. I was also able to choose from a broad range of courses that aligned closely with my interests in understanding human behavior, society, and global issues, areas that are central to social sciences. The platform’s accessible resources and the ability to review lessons on demand helped reinforce my learning and boosted my academic performance.***

***More than just an online school, OVS fostered the kind of independent thinking, academic discipline, and critical reflection that I know will serve me well in McMaster’s Social Sciences program. It laid a strong foundation not just for academic success, but for the kind of thoughtful, engaged learning that McMaster encourages. I truly believe my experience with OVS has equipped me with the mindset and skills to thrive in university and beyond.”***

![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/mcmaster-university.png)

We are very excited to see another one of our students achieve success in their academic career and we wish Alaina the best of luck in her program next year!

## Previous Winner: Eric Manikhouth

***“By offering a flexible, customized, and excellent learning environment, Ontario Virtual School (OVS) was super helpful for me to reach my academic goals. OVS let me study at my own speed as an online platform, which helped me especially balance my studies with other commitments. While the skilled and encouraging teachers gave clear instruction and timely feedback, guaranteeing I completely understood the content, the vast choice of courses offered allowed me to study subjects that matched my interests and future objectives. Furthermore, the availability of tools and the possibility to review courses helped strengthen my knowledge and raise my performance. OVS’s focus on self-discipline and independent learning also helped me to be ready for the challenges of university by preparing me with the tools required to succeed in my university career. All things considered, OVS was crucial in allowing me to reach my academic potential and realize my goals.”***

![Eric Manikhouth](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Eric-Photo-300x605.jpg)

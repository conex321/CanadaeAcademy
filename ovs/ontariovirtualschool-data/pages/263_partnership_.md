# Partnerships | Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/partnership
**Description:** There are various partnership opportunities available with Ontario Virtual School. Contact us at any time to learn more about OVS partnerships.

---

Ontario Virtual School is a Ministry approved OSSD credit granting institution that offers high school level courses from Grade 6 through 12 in an online format. We are currently in our 15th year of operation and have grown to support and promote the learning of over 25,000 students annually.![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/shutterstock_793972774-500x337.jpg)

### Why OVS

* Register and start at anytime from anywhere worldwide
* Students can take courses without a student permit
* Customized admin portal for all partner schools to monitor their students
* Over 170 different courses offered
* Branded partner logo on the students’ Learning Management system
* All levels of courses offered (University, College, Mixed, Open)
* Complete courses as quickly as 4 weeks
* All courses taught by Ontario Certified Teachers
* Marks sent directly to partner school and/or OUAC
* 25,000+ enrolled students
* Regular update of achievement to parents & counsellors
* Exceptional service, with marks within 48 hours
* Personal school liaison assigned
* 4.9 rating on Google reviews

### Nordic Hockey Academy

Location: Norway

Worldwide respected Nordic “Scandinavian” spirit and pedagogical development approach is adapted into everyday working culture and training environment in the Academy.

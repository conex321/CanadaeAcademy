# New Course Offerings - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/new-course-offerings
**Description:** We’ve added several new courses this Fall – check them out here!

---

![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/new-course-offerings-informal-500x500.png)

We’ve added several new courses this Fall in Business, Technology, Health and Human Development! Click on any of the links below for more details.

[BBI1O](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/bbi1o/) – Grade 9 Introduction to Business
[BBI2O](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/bbi2o/) – Grade 10 Introduction to Business
[BDI3C](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/bdi3c/) – Grade 11 Entrepreneurship
[BOH4M](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/boh4m/) – Grade 12 Business Leadership
[ICS3U](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/ics3u/) – Grade 11 Computer Science
[TPJ3M](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/tpj3m/) – Grade 11 Healthcare Technology
[HFA4U](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/hfa4u/) – Grade 12 Nutrition and Health
[HHG4M](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/hhg4m/) – Grade 12 Human Growth and Development Throughout the Lifespan

Psst: HFA4U and HHG4M are excellent elective options for students looking to round out their top 6 for university applications!

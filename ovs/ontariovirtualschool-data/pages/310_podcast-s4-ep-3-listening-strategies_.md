In this episode, OVS teacher and podcast host Alicia Cuzner discusses the topic of listening and how it’s an active process.

This episode explores different ways to optimize your time for effective learning and defines the process of listening to help you better understand why it’s important, even when the topic doesn’t initially interest you.

The episode also features a Student Spotlight with recent OVS graduate Alexandra Toupikov, who serves as Ontario Virtual School’s Book Club leader and coordinator. Alexandra shares a variety of strategies for listening in different situations, whether online or in person.

For more learning resources, or to register for high school courses, please visit [www.OntarioVirtualSchool.ca](http://www.OntarioVirtualSchool.ca). Check us out on all platforms by searching the handle @ontariovirtualschool.

# 2020 Holiday Hours - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/2020-holiday-hours
**Description:** We will be closed on Christmas Day and New Year’s Day, and will be closing at 4pm on Christmas Eve and New Year’s Eve. All other weekdays will be regular hours.

---

![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Office-Hours-500x500.jpg)
The OVS office will be open remotely throughout the holiday season to better support you as you work through your courses over the Winter break.

We will be closed on Christmas Day and New Year’s Day, and will be closing at 4pm on Christmas Eve and New Year’s Eve. All other weekdays will be regular hours, with phone, email, and chat support available from 8am-8pm.

Please note, these are office hours only. Individual teachers will be updating their course homepages with any expected delays in their holiday marking schedule. Please be sure to check your course homepages for these notices!

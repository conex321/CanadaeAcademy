#### What Grade 12 courses are offered online at Ontario Virtual School?

Ontario Virtual School offers a full list of Grade 12 courses in Ontario, including English, Advanced Functions, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Business Leadership, and more. All courses are 100% online and count toward your Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD).

#### Are Grade 12 online courses accepted by universities and colleges?

Yes. All Ontario Grade 12 courses at OVS are fully accredited by the Ontario Ministry of Education and recognized by universities and colleges across Canada and internationally.

#### Can I complete Grade 12 courses online at my own pace?

Absolutely. OVS allows you to start anytime and learn at your own pace. You can complete your online Grade 12 courses in as little as four weeks or take up to 12 months — the choice is yours.

#### How do I enroll in Grade 12 online courses with Ontario Virtual School?

You can easily register online. Simply select your desired course from the list of Grade 12 courses above, add it to your cart, and follow the checkout steps to begin learning.

#### What are the benefits of taking Grade 12 courses online in Ontario?

Students enjoy flexible scheduling, self-paced learning, and access to certified Ontario teachers. OVS’s Grade 12 online courses also help students balance academics with extracurriculars or part-time work while earning OSSD credits.

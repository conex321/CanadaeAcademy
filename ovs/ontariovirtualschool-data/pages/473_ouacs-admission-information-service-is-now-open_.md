![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1.jpg)

Haven’t received an offer of admission? OUAC’s Admission Information Service (AIS) is here to help!

The AIS allows you to search for programs across all Ontario universities that still have spaces available.

Visit [OUAC’s 101 webpage](https://www.ouac.on.ca/ouac-101/) and click “Search Open Programs” to access this powerful tool.

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/AIS-Flyer.jpg)](https://www.ouac.on.ca/ouac-101/)

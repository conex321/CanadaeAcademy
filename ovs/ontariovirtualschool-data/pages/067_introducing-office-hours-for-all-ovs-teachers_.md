# Introducing Teacher Office Hours - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/introducing-office-hours-for-all-ovs-teachers
**Description:** All OVS teachers will now be hosting weekly office hours! During these hours, students can expect near-immediate responses to their email inquiries.

---

![OVS teacher office hours](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Introducing-Office-Hours-for-Teachers-1-500x375.png)

At OVS, we constantly strive to provide our students with the best possible learning experience we can. That is why we are proud to announce that, effective immediately, in addition to our regular 24-hour email response time, all OVS teachers will now be holding weekly office hours! These hours will be set by the individual instructor and can be found on the right-hand side of your course homepage. During these times, students can expect a prompt response to their email inquiries.

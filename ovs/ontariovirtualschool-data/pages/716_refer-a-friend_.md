Do you have a friend or know of a student who might be interested in taking a course with OVS? Use this form to refer them to us! When you do, an info packet will be emailed to the student starting with the following: “We write to you at the request of [your name here] who thought you might be interested in learning more about Ontario Virtual School.” If you have any questions, please call 416-907-4899 or email [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection) to speak with our staff. \***Refer a friend to OVS and you get a $50 Amazon.ca gift card!** If you’re an existing student, you can refer your friends or family to Ontario Virtual School. For every new referral who registers with us (paying the regular fee), you will receive this limited promotional gift.

\*New students **must** indicate the referring person **on the registration form** to authenticate this offer.

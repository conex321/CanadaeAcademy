# Privacy Policy & Intellectual Property | Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/privacy-policy
**Description:** OVS is committed to the security & privacy of each student, client, associate & website visitor. Read our privacy & intellectual property policy for details.

---

**Last Updated:** July 23, 2025
Ontario Virtual School Inc. (“OVS”, “we”, “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of our students, clients, associates, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

By using our site or registering for services, you consent to the terms of this policy.

## 1. What Information We Collect

Personal Identification Information

We may collect personal information from users in a variety of ways, including when users:

* Visit our website
* Register for courses
* Submit a form
* Complete payments
* Contact us for support

Information collected may include:

* Name
* Email address
* Mailing address
* Phone number
* Date of birth
* Academic records
* Payment details

We only collect personal information that is voluntarily submitted, and users can choose not to supply it, although this may limit access to certain services.

Non-Personal Identification Information

We may also collect information such as:

* Browser type and version
* IP address
* Device type and operating system
* Usage patterns on our website

## 2. Web Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience. These cookies help remember user preferences and track anonymized web analytics via Google Analytics. This tool collects usage data (e.g., browser, IP, pages visited), but does not personally identify users.

Users can disable cookies in their browser settings or install Google’s Analytics Opt-Out Tool.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

* Operate and maintain our website and LMS
* Process course registrations and payments
* Deliver online learning services and academic records
* Provide customer support
* Communicate updates, schedules, and service notifications
* Processing payments
* Meeting legal and/or regulatory education requirements
* Improve our services and content

## 4. Children’s Privacy

We accept students under the age of 13. In such cases, parental or guardian consent is required to register and participate. We collect only the information necessary to deliver educational services and take extra care to protect the privacy of minors.

Parents/guardians may contact us at any time to access, correct, or request deletion of their child’s information.

## 5. How We Protect Your Information

We implement strong administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect your data, including:

* SSL encryption for all data transmissions
* Role-based access control and 2FA for staff
* Encrypted storage on secure Canadian and U.S.-based servers
* Regular security audits and backups

## 6. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your information may be stored in:

* Canada – Primary servers and internal systems
* United States – Limited use of third-party tools or backups

We only use service providers that meet high standards of data security and confidentiality.

## 7. Third-Party Services

We may share limited personal information with trusted third-party vendors solely for the purpose of delivering our services. These may include:

* PayPal – Secure payment processing
* Google Analytics – Anonymized website usage tracking
* Cloud and backup providers – Secure hosting and storage
* Internal OVS LMS – Course delivery and student records

All third-party providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and comply with applicable privacy laws.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

## 8. Retention of Personal Information

We retain academic records and payment history as required by provincial regulations, typically:

* Academic records: 7 years
* Inactive accounts: up to 2 years
* Communications and support requests: as needed for service delivery

Information is securely deleted or anonymized once it is no longer required.

## 9. Access, Correction & Consent Withdrawal

Under PIPEDA, you have the right to:

* Request access to your personal data
* Correct inaccurate or outdated information
* Withdraw consent (where applicable)

To make such requests, please contact: [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)

## 10. Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach involving your personal information, OVS will:

* Promptly notify affected individuals
* Report to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada, if legally required
* Take corrective action to prevent recurrence

## 11. Newsletters and Promotions

If you opt in, we may send you occasional updates, newsletters, or promotional information. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in the email or by contacting us directly.

## 12. Location Data

If you use features such as our registration form, we may ask you to confirm your location (e.g., to calculate local fees). This information is used only for service delivery and internal analysis.

## 13. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage users to review third-party policies when visiting external links.

## 14. Changes to this Policy

OVS may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will post the revised version with the updated date. For active students, we may also send a notification via email or LMS.

We encourage users to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.

Are you a student looking for more control over your education? Are you busy with sports, a job, or hoping to graduate early? Consider earning your OSSD credits through an accredited self- paced online high school—[Ontario Virtual School (OVS).](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/)

A traditional school schedule doesn’t work for everyone. Online high school offers the flexibility to start anytime and learn at your own pace, while still meeting [OSSD requirements](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ossd-requirements/).

## Start Anytime

At OVS, students can:

* + - Enroll in courses year-round
    - Start learning immediately
    - Receive marked assignments within 48 hours
    - Work through course at their own pace

> “ With OVS, I was able to complete assignments on my schedule, and graduate early!”
> – Tomas V., OVS Student

![Self-Paced (1)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Self-Paced-1.gif)

## Immediate Feedback

At OVS, students benefit from:

* + - Feedback from teachers within 48 hours
    - The ability to submit drafts prior to final submissions
    - Daily access to teachers via email during designated office hours
    - Quick grading and responses

> “ I appreciate receiving timely feedback on my drafts from Ms. Y at OVS, as it helps me refine my work and re-submit for final grading. I love getting my marks back so quickly!”
> – Aaminah H., OVS Student

![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Feedback.gif)

## Tailored Learning

At OVS:

* Students with anxiety find a flexible, [low-stress learning environment](https://smho-smso.ca/about-student-mental-health-in-ontario/)
* Allowances for [IEP accommodations](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/iep-accommodations/)
* Adult students can earn high school credits while working full-time
* International students can meet Ontario graduation requirements
* Students with irregular schedules (i.e. sports, travel, health) are supported
* Students can [upgrade high school marks](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/upgrade-courses/) online to improve post-secondary applications

![Tailored Learning](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Tailored-Learning.gif)

> “One of my students in [HFA4U](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/courses/HFA4U/), had a change in their career pathway. They were able to take the appropriate courses online without delaying their graduation date.”
> -Ms. Y, OVS Teacher

## Conclusion

The flexible learning environment at OVS is paired with quality and standards you can trust. Ontario Virtual School is a **self-paced online high school**, fully aligned with the [Ontario Ministry of Education](https://www.ontario.ca/page/education-ontario), taught by certified Ontario Teachers and aligns directly with the [Ontario Curriculum](https://www.dcp.edu.gov.on.ca/en/curriculum#secondary). Whether you need one course or a full **Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD)**, your learning will be supported at Ontario Virtual School (OVS).

Start your journey to academic success at OVS today. Explore course offerings and register today to earn your Ontario high school diploma.

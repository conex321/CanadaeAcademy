In this episode Alicia Cuzner and Dwayne Matthews will discuss what an [IEP](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/iep-accommodations/) is, and the misconceptions behind IEPs. Our hosts unpack the challenges behind getting an IEP made, as well as the stigmas behind IEPs and learning disabilities. Listen to find our more about what an IEP is, and how everyone should have one. This episode also includes a Post Secondary Spotlight on the [University of Toronto, Mississauga Campus](https://www.utm.utoronto.ca/), and an interview with the U of T Diversity Recruitment and Admissions Officer, Oje Izerein.

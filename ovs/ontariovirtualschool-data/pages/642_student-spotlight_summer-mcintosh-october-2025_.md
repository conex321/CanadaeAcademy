Summer has been a student with OVS since 2020, completing high school courses alongside her athletic training and competitions.

## 2025 World Aquatics Championship

The 2025 World Aquatics Championship took places in Singapore from July to August this year.

Summer raced in five individual events and medaled in each one.

She earned Gold in the 400m freestyle with a time of 3:56.26.

She achieved her second Gold medal in the 200m Individual Medley with a time of 2:06.69.

She claimed her third Gold medal for the 200M Butterfly with a time of 2:01.99

Her final Gold came from the 400m Individual Medley where she broke her own competition record with a time of 4:25.78.

Finally, she earned a bronze medal in the 800m Freestyle with a time of 4:25.78, coming ins just 1.67 seconds behind gold medalist American swimmer Katie Ledecky.

## Summer McIntosh OVS Scholarship

![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/OVS-Summer-Scholarship-1024x236.png)

OVS is proud of being a part of Summer’s incredible journey. As such, to help out more of our student athletes we are offering a yearly scholarship in Summer’s name.

This scholarship was created to celebrate the achievements of an Ontario high-performance athlete who has competed at the provincial, national, and/or international level, and is designed to offset the expenses of pursuing post-secondary education. The scholarship is valued as $5000.

We are excited for Summer and everything that she has accomplished and are happy to have helped her achieve her high school diploma while also following her passions for swimming. We can’t wait to see what more Summer will accomplish and are thrilled to have her as one of our OVS alumni.

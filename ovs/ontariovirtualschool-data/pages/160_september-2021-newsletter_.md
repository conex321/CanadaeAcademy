# September 2021 Newsletter - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/september-2021-newsletter
**Description:** It's that time again! OVS is here to ensure a smooth transition back to school this fall with info on registration, scheduling, and post-secondary!

As families across the province weigh the pros and cons of online versus in-person learning, many continue to seek out alternative options to ensure a safe, reliable return to school.

As [a Ministry-inspected school](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/what-ministry-inspected-means-and-why-it-matters/) with more than a decade of online-only experience, OVS offers an easy-to-use platform that supports multiple learning styles. Our self-paced, fully online courses alleviate concerns surrounding in-person contact, mask and vaccination requirements, as well as inconvenient or unreliable scheduling.

Get in touch with our office today to inquire about switching to OVS full-time, or [check our website for more information](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/high-school-9-12/)!

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Safe-September-500x419.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Safe-September.jpg)

Getting back into a routine can be tough! [Check out our website for helpful tips from our Student Services team](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/guidance-student-success/) that will help you set yourself up for success by designing a study schedule that works for you. Or, get in touch with our Guidance and Student Support teams to ensure a smooth transition back this Fall.

## OVS x Labster – Partners in Educational Innovation

OVS is proud to partner with [Labster](https://www.labster.com/), the world’s leading provider of virtual science labs, to bring innovative and interactive virtual lab experiences to each of our 15 science courses, from Grades 9 through 12!

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/labster-1024x502.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/labster.jpg)

Labster’s virtual lab simulations offer an immersive 3D learning environment where students can complete interactive laboratory experiments online. These simulations allow students to work through real-life case stories, interact with lab equipment, perform experiments, and explore scientific concepts, procedures, and theories in a uniquely interactive setting – without ever leaving their home!

To learn more about Labster’s innovative virtual lab technology, [watch the video below](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UEW4JeVwvps&t=2s).

## Planning Your Path to Post-Secondary

Check out [our blog](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/save-the-date-ontario-universities-fair/) for university fair information for across Canada, UK, USA.

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouinfo_tv_ad_connect_the_dots-500x281.png)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouinfo_tv_ad_connect_the_dots.png)

Be sure to mark your calendar for the virtual Ontario Universities’ Fairs (OUFs)! This year’s OUFs will consist of 2 virtual events held on:
• Saturday, September 25, 12-2 pm (ET)
• Tuesday, October 26, 5-7 pm (ET)

All Ontario universities will participate in these free events and anyone may attend – pre-registration is not required. Each event will be divided up into four 30-minute sessions, where students can learn about the universities of their choice and ask questions.

These events are a great opportunity to get answers about programs, admission requirements, student life and much more! To learn more about the OUFs at: [www.ontariouniversitiesfair.ca](https://www.ontariouniversitiesfair.ca/).

OVS Tip: Don’t forget to check out our blog on  [Understanding the OUAC Application Process](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/understanding-the-2021-ouac-application-process/) , as well as our [post-secondary pathways series](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/post-secondary-pathways/) to help you plan your best path forward!

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/free-course-images/OVS5O.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/free-course-images/OVS5O.jpg)

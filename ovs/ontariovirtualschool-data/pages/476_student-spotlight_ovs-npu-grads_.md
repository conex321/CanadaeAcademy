![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/NPU-OVS-Grads-2019-500x372.jpg)
Thanks to [our partnership with Northern Pre University](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/partner/northern-pre-university-npu/), OVS has helped dozens of Quebec students earn an [Ontario Secondary School Diploma](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ossd-requirements/)  in a flexible format that works for their busy extra-curricular schedules, while still ensuring they follow a path to success in post-secondary. Keep scrolling to see where a few of our joint graduates from 2019-2020 ended up!

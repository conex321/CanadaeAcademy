# Office Closure Update - Ontario Virtual School

**Source:** https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/office-closure-update
**Description:** Effective November 23rd - January 4th, our Yonge St. office will be closed for all in-person inquiries. Remote support will continue to be available.

---

![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/COVID-19-UPDATE-500x419.jpg)
Please note, as of Monday, November 23rd, our office has elected to close until the new year. We will continue to receive regular mail and textbook returns.

At this time, in-person consultations will not be available. Our staff will still be available by phone and email during regular office hours (8am-8pm, Monday to Friday).

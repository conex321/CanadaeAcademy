In this episode, podcast host and Ontario Virtual School teacher, Alicia Cuzner, will discuss strategies that students who are graduating from high school can use when entering post secondary schools. Joining her this episode is Professor Jennifer Davis, from Queen’s University. Since 2010, Dr. Davis has been a Professor with the Faculty of Education and has worked as a student success coordinator for the World Indigenous Studies in Education (WISE) program. This episode explores the common trends that Dr. Davis has seen when teaching her first-year students, as well as appropriate ways to incorporate AI into learning. For more school resources, please visit www.OntarioVirtualSchool.ca or check us out on all platforms by searching @ontariovirtualschool

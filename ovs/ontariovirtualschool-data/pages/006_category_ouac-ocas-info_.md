OUAC Updates With the start of a new school year comes a new university application season. It is essential for students to not only understand which applicant type they are, but to be aware of all upcoming OUAC applications deadlines.  Which Applicant Group Am I? First, here are some important basics for navigating the OUAC […](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ouac-updates/)

[![ouf_logo_grey_box](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouf_logo_grey_box-305x220.png)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ontario-university-fairs/ "Ontario University Fairs")

    The OUF takes place from September 27th to 28th 2025, at the Metro Toronto Convention Centre (MTCC) in the South Building. The fair runs from 9:30am to 5pm both days and offers free admission. This is a great opportunity for current high school students to talk with representatives from different universities and to really start thinking […](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ontario-university-fairs/)

[![OUAC collection](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/OUAC-MIDTERMS-1-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ouacs-first-collection-period-is-now-open/ "OUAC Collection is Now Open for 2022-23 Applicants!")

OUAC’s first collection period for 2022 applicants is now open for first semester midterms and any finals achieved in Grade 11 or 12 courses.

[![ON university fair](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/OUF-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/save-the-date-ontario-universities-fair/ "Save the Date: University Fairs!")

Are you planning to apply to university in 2022? Then be sure to mark your calendar for the Ontario Universities’ Fairs happening this fall!

[![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ouacs-admission-information-service-is-now-live/ "OUAC’s Admission Information Service is Now Live!")

OUAC’s AIS allows last-minute applicants to search for university programs across Ontario with spaces available for Fall 2021.

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Which-Group-Am-I-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/finals-season-which-group-am-i/ "Finals Season: Which Group Am I?")

During finals season, every OVS student falls into one of the three groups. Find out which one you are so you can follow the correct deadlines!

[![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/responding-to-an-offer-on-ouac/ "Responding to an Offer on OUAC")

Have you received an offer from a university? Not sure what to do next? We’ve got you covered!

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/Copy-of-OUAC-MIDTERMS-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ouacs-last-midterm-collection-for-2021-is-now-open/ "OUAC’s last midterm collection is now open!")

OUAC’s last midterm collection period will run from March 15th through until April 22nd. If you haven’t yet submitted your midterms for universities to view, be sure to read this!

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/OUAC-MIDTERMS-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/midterm-collection-2/ "Midterm Collection #2")

OUAC will be collecting midterm marks from January 18th through until February 11th. More information here!

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/UPDATED-OUAC-With-Border-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/reminder-create-your-ouac-account-by-january-15th/ "Reminder: Create Your OUAC Account by January 15th!")

Have you applied through OUAC yet? January 15th is the deadline to use your PIN to create your account & apply to your chosen schools!

[![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/understanding-the-2021-ouac-application/ "OUAC’s 2021 Tutorial Videos")

Click through for easy-to-follow tutorials taking you through the application process, including how to ensure your OVS courses are listed!

[![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/winter-spring-admissions-dates-for-101-applicants/ "Winter/Spring Admissions Dates for 101 Applicants")

OUAC has released their dates and eligibility conditions for Winter and Spring applications, click through for the link!

[![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ouacs-2020-collection-has-closed/ "OUAC’s 2020 Collection Has Closed")

The 101 application for admission in 2020 is now closed. The 2021 application will be available in early October.

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/logo-einfo-2x-305x204.png)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ontario-universities-info-2021-is-live/ "Ontario Universities’ Info 2021 is live!")

Ontario Universities’ Info 2021 is live! Find and compare Ontario university programs; research residences, scholarships, admission requirements and more.

[![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ouac-summer-collection/ "OUAC Summer Collection")

OUAC’s 2020 Summer Collection for 101 Applicants closes August 31st. Students who need a final mark from OVS submitted must follow these deadlines.

[![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/ouacs-admission-information-service-is-now-open/ "OUAC’s Admission Information Service is Now Open!")

Haven’t received an offer of admission? OUAC’s Admission Information Service (AIS) is here to help!

[![](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/OUF-1-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/2020-ontario-university-fair-cancelled/ "Ontario University Fair Cancelled")

The OUAC, on behalf of the SCSR and the OUF Steering Committee, has announced the cancellation of this year’s Ontario University Fair as a result of COVID-19.

[![OUAC Ontario Universities Application Centre](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/wp-content/uploads/ouac-1-305x220.jpg)](https://www.ontariovirtualschool.ca/new-ouac-covid-19-update/ "OUAC Deadline Extension")

OUAC announced today that they will be extending their midterm collection deadline until May 1st. Any OVS student who submits a midterm request before April 30th will have their mark submitted to OUAC. Please consult with your instructor to ensure you meet this new deadline.
